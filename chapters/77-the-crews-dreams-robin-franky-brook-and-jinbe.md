# Chapter 77: The Crew's Dreams - Robin, Franky, Brook, and Jinbe

- **Setting:** Ohara (rebuilt), Water 7, the Twin Capes, Fishman Island (surface)
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Robin, Franky, Brook, Jinbe, Laboon
