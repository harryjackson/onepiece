# Chapter 28: Amazon Lily - The Island of Women

- **Setting:** Amazon Lily — an island of warrior women in the Calm Belt
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Boa Hancock (Snake Princess, Warlord), Boa Sandersonia, Boa Marigold, Marguerite, Elder Nyon, the Kuja Pirates
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Crash Landing** — Harry smashes through the jungle canopy and lands on Amazon Lily. He is battered, exhausted, and alone.
  2. **Captured by the Kuja** — Marguerite and Kuja warriors find Harry. They have never seen a man before. They poke his rubber skin in wonder.
  3. **The Arena** — Harry is brought before Boa Hancock. She uses her Love-Love Fruit power — Mero Mero Mellow — which turns anyone who feels attraction into stone. She aims it at Harry. Nothing happens. Harry is too simple and innocent to be affected.
  4. **Battle with the Gorgon Sisters** — Hancock orders her sisters to fight Harry. Both use Haki — a power Harry has never encountered. During the battle, Harry covers Sandersonia's back with his own body, protecting a secret.
  5. **The Secret on Their Backs** — Hancock reveals the Hoof of the Soaring Dragon — the brand of a Celestial Dragon slave. All three sisters were enslaved as children. Fisher Tiger freed them.
  6. **Harry's Fury** — Harry is furious at the Celestial Dragons: "I already punched one of those guys!" Hancock begins to fall in love with Harry.
  7. **The Terrible News** — Harry's brother Ace has been captured and is scheduled for public execution at Marineford. Harry goes pale. "I have to go save him. Right now."
  8. **Hancock's Decision** — Hancock offers to smuggle Harry into Impel Down on the Marine warship. It is insane. Harry agrees instantly.
- **Emotional Beat:** Hancock's slave backstory. Harry's immediate rage on her behalf. The news about Ace shattering Harry's world.
- **Anime Moments:** Hancock's Mero Mero Mellow bouncing off Harry, the brand on Hancock's back, Harry's face when he reads about Ace
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry alone, Hancock as an ally. Crew still scattered.
