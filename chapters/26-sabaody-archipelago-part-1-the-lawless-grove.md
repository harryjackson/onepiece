# Chapter 26: Sabaody Archipelago (Part 1) - The Lawless Grove

- **Setting:** Sabaody Archipelago — an island of mangrove trees near the Red Line
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Silvers Rayleigh (Roger's first mate), the Eleven Supernovas, Celestial Dragons, Admiral Kizaru
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arrival at the Archipelago** — The Thousand Sunny surfaces near the massive mangrove trees of Sabaody. Bubbles float everywhere. The crew explores. This is the last stop before the New World.
  2. **Duval and the Flying Fish Riders** — Duval's face matches Sanji's terrible wanted poster sketch. Sanji kicks his face into a new shape — Duval becomes handsome and pledges his Flying Fish Riders to the crew.
  3. **Meeting Camie, Hachi, and Pappag** — The crew reunites with Hachi — the octopus fishman from Arlong Park, now reformed. He introduces Camie (a mermaid) and Pappag (a talking starfish). Hachi warns: "Do not anger the Celestial Dragons."
  4. **Exploring the Lawless Zone** — The crew splits up. Harry, Chopper, and Brook ride bubble bikes. Nami and Robin notice slave collars on sale in a shop. Zoro immediately gets lost.
  5. **The Eleven Supernovas** — Harry bumps into other rookie pirates. Eustass Kid, Trafalgar Law, Jewelry Bonney, Basil Hawkins, and more. Each radiates danger.
  6. **Shakky's Rip-Off Bar** — Hachi leads part of the crew to Shakky's bar. Her partner is Silvers Rayleigh — the Dark King, first mate of Gold Roger.
  7. **The Slave Auction House** — Camie is kidnapped by slave traders. Celestial Dragons sit in VIP seats, bidding on slaves. It is horrifying. Hachi begs them not to cause a scene.
  8. **Hachi Is Shot** — Hachi rushes the stage to save Camie, revealing himself as a fishman. Saint Charlos shoots him in the chest. Everyone stares at Harry.
  9. **Harry Punches a God** — Harry walks forward. He punches Saint Charlos in the face. The Celestial Dragon crashes through a wall. Dead silence. Then chaos.
  10. **Rayleigh Appears** — An old man emerges from backstage. He releases Conqueror's Haki, knocking out every guard. This is Silvers Rayleigh, first mate of the Pirate King. He frees Camie's explosive collar.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry punching the Celestial Dragon — standing against the most powerful bloodline in the world because they hurt his friend.
- **Anime Moments:** The Eleven Supernovas assembled, Harry's fist connecting with Saint Charlos's face, Rayleigh unleashing Conqueror's Haki
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook — Marine Admiral incoming
