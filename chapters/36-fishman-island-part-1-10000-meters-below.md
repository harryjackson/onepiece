# Chapter 36: Fishman Island (Part 1) - 10,000 Meters Below

- **Setting:** Fishman Island — an underwater paradise beneath the Red Line
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Jinbe, Shirahoshi (mermaid princess), Hody Jones, Vander Decken, Neptune
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arrival at Fishman Island** — The crew descends into the underwater kingdom. Sanji nearly dies of happiness seeing mermaids.
  2. **Exploring the Kingdom** — Harry rides giant fish. Chopper bonds with sea creatures. Brook asks a mermaid to show him her panties.
  3. **Shirahoshi** — Harry meets Princess Shirahoshi — a mermaid so enormous she fills the room. She has been locked in a tower for ten years. Harry calls her a crybaby but offers to take her outside.
  4. **Hody Jones** — A fishman supremacist seizes Fishman Island. His hatred was taught, not earned. He takes Energy Steroids and threatens to execute Neptune.
  5. **Fisher Tiger's Story** — Centuries of slavery against fishmen. Fisher Tiger climbed the Red Line with bare hands, freed every slave in Mariejois, but refused a human blood transfusion and died. "Do not teach the children to hate."
  6. **Queen Otohime's Dream** — Otohime dreamed of coexistence. She was assassinated — Hody Jones arranged it to keep the hatred alive.
  7. **Shirahoshi's Strength** — Harry recognizes Shirahoshi's strength — the strength to endure and not seek vengeance. She cries different tears — of being understood.
- **Emotional Beat:** Fisher Tiger refusing human blood. Otohime's assassination. Shirahoshi's decade of silence.
- **Anime Moments:** Fishman Island revealed, Fisher Tiger climbing the Red Line, Queen Otohime being shot
