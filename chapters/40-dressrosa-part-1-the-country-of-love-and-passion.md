# Chapter 40: Dressrosa (Part 1) - The Country of Love and Passion

- **Setting:** Dressrosa — a beautiful kingdom secretly ruled by terror
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Law, Doflamingo (Joker), the Donquixote Family, Rebecca, the dwarves (Tontattas), Sabo
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arrival in Dressrosa** — A colorful Spanish-inspired kingdom. Toys walk alongside humans and no one finds it strange.
  2. **Doflamingo's Kingdom** — Beneath the beauty, Dressrosa is rotten. Doflamingo rules with invisible strings. He is a former Celestial Dragon.
  3. **The Toy Curse** — Sugar's Hobby-Hobby Fruit turns people into toys and erases all memory of them. The cruelest power imaginable.
  4. **The Corrida Colosseum** — The prize: the Flame-Flame Fruit — Ace's old Devil Fruit. Harry enters the tournament under a terrible disguise.
  5. **Rebecca** — A gladiator princess fighting to win the Flame-Flame Fruit and free her father, who was turned into a toy.
  6. **SABO APPEARS** — A man in a top hat enters the tournament. It is Sabo — Ace and Harry's other brother, thought dead. He survived, lost his memory, was raised by Dragon. When he heard about Ace's death, his memories returned. Harry cries. They embrace.
  7. **Sabo Inherits the Flame** — Sabo wins the tournament and eats the Flame-Flame Fruit. Fire erupts from his fists — Ace's fire. "I will carry your fire, Ace. I promise."
  8. **The Dwarves and the Resistance** — The crew discovers the Tontattas — tiny dwarves enslaved by Doflamingo's forces. Usopp is mistaken for a legendary hero.
- **Emotional Beat:** Sabo's return — the brother they thought was dead. Sabo eating Ace's fruit and carrying his will. The toy curse. Rebecca fighting for a forgotten father.
- **Anime Moments:** The Corrida Colosseum battles, Sabo revealing himself to Harry, Sabo's fist erupting in Ace's fire
