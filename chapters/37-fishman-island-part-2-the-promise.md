# Chapter 37: Fishman Island (Part 2) - The Promise

- **Setting:** Fishman Island
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Jinbe, Hody Jones, Shirahoshi, Big Mom (Den Den Mushi)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **100,000 Enemies** — Harry releases Conqueror's Haki — 50,000 fishmen collapse. The remaining 50,000 stare in terror.
  2. **The Crew Fights** — Each crew member takes on thousands. Every crew member is dramatically stronger.
  3. **Vander Decken's Attack** — Noah is hurled at Fishman Island. Sea Kings appear, summoned by Shirahoshi — she is Poseidon.
  4. **Harry vs Hody Jones** — Harry overwhelms Hody even underwater. The vast gulf between post-timeskip Harry and a Grand Line villain.
  5. **Breaking the Cycle** — Harry gives blood to save a fishman. A human giving blood freely. The cycle of hatred cracks.
  6. **Harry Claims Fishman Island** — Harry declares Fishman Island his territory and plants his flag. Jinbe promises to formally join the crew.
  7. **Big Mom Calls** — Big Mom — one of the Four Emperors — threatens Harry. Harry: "I am going to kick your ass!" He hangs up.
  8. **Farewell to Fishman Island** — The crew feasts with the fishmen. The Sunny rises toward the New World.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry giving blood — breaking the cycle Fisher Tiger could not. Standing up to Big Mom.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry knocking out 50,000 with Conqueror's Haki, Sea Kings answering Shirahoshi, Harry telling Big Mom he is coming for her
- **Crew After Chapter:** Full crew (Jinbe promised to join)
