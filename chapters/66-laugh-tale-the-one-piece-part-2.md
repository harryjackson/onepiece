# Chapter 66: Laugh Tale - The One Piece (Part 2)

- **Setting:** Laugh Tale — the aftermath, then departure
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Shanks, Blackbeard (final confrontation)
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **Harry vs. Blackbeard — The Final Round** — Teach attacks again. Joy of freedom vs. hunger for power.
  2. **Teach's Power Unleashed** — Darkness negates devil fruit powers. Harry is forced out of Gear 5 and fights with pure Haki.
  3. **Harry's Answer** — Harry laughs in the darkness. "I was a rubber kid before I was a god." His Haki overloads Teach's darkness. Gear 5 reignites.
  4. **The Final Blow** — A Gear 5 punch imbued with supreme Conqueror's Haki. Blackbeard is broken.
  5. **Teach's Retreat** — The Blackbeard Pirates flee. For the first time, Teach looks truly afraid.
  6. **Shanks and Harry — The Reunion** — They share sake on the beach. Shanks: "Roger would have liked you." Harry tries to give the straw hat back. Shanks: "Not yet. When the world is free."
  7. **Shanks's Story** — Shanks reveals he saw the One Piece as a child on Roger's crew. "It was always going to be someone who could make everyone around them smile."
  8. **The War Council** — To break the Red Line, they need all three Ancient Weapons. To get Uranus, they must assault Mary Geoise. Total war.
  9. **Messages Sent** — Calls to every ally: Vivi, Momonosuke, Shirahoshi, Sabo, the Grand Fleet.
  10. **Departure from Laugh Tale** — Harry carves his mark next to Roger's. "WE'LL BE BACK."
  11. **News Spreads** — Harry is declared King of the Pirates. Imu: "Prepare the cleansing."
  12. **Sailing to War** — Harry stands at the prow. "Let's go change the world."
- **Emotional Beat:** Shanks and Harry sharing sake at Laugh Tale. The man who gave Harry his dream watching it fulfilled.
- **Anime Moments:** Gear 5 blazing through darkness, Shanks and Harry sharing sake, Harry carving his mark next to Roger's
