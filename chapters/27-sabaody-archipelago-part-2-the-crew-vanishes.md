# Chapter 27: Sabaody Archipelago (Part 2) - The Crew Vanishes

- **Setting:** Sabaody Archipelago — the groves becoming a war zone
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Kizaru (Admiral, Light-Light Fruit), Sentomaru, Pacifistas, Bartholomew Kuma, Rayleigh
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Rayleigh's Offer** — Rayleigh tells the crew about Roger. Harry stops him: "I do not want to know. We will find it ourselves." Rayleigh agrees to coat their ship. Three days they may not have.
  2. **Admiral Kizaru Arrives** — A beam of light shoots down and explodes a mangrove tree. Admiral Kizaru — the Light-Light Fruit. He moves at the speed of light. He is completely untouchable.
  3. **Sentomaru and the Pacifistas** — Pacifista cyborg replicas of Kuma fire laser beams. The crew barely brings one down together. Then a second appears. And a third.
  4. **Scattered and Desperate** — Kizaru appears in front of Zoro and casually kicks him through trees. Rayleigh blocks Kizaru's next attack with Haki: "Go! Take your crew and run!"
  5. **The Real Kuma Appears** — Kuma touches Zoro. Zoro vanishes — launched to an unknown island. The crew screams.
  6. **One by One** — Kuma moves methodically. Brook — gone. Usopp — gone. Sanji — gone. Franky — gone. Nami runs crying, reaching for Harry — gone. Chopper — gone. Robin — gone. Harry watches each one disappear, screaming their names.
  7. **Harry Is Last** — Harry stands alone, panting, crying. Kuma walks toward him. Harry collapses to his knees: "I could not save a single one of them..." Kuma touches Harry. The world stretches. Harry is launched into the sky.
  8. **Alone in the Sky** — Harry flies for three days and three nights. He replays the moment — every crew member vanishing, one by one. The boy who would be King of the Pirates could not save a single friend.
- **Emotional Beat:** The crew vanishing one by one. Harry reaching for each person and failing. The silence after the last one disappears. This is rock bottom.
- **Anime Moments:** Kuma touching each crew member as they vanish, Harry on his knees alone in the empty grove, Harry flying through the sky
- **Crew After Chapter:** SCATTERED. Every member on a different island. Harry is alone.
