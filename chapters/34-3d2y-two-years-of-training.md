# Chapter 34: 3D2Y - Two Years of Training

- **Setting:** Various islands; primarily Rusukaina (Harry), plus glimpses of each crew member's training location
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Rayleigh, Jinbe (flashback), each crew member briefly
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Waking Up Broken** — Harry wakes on Amazon Lily. The first thing he remembers is Ace. He screams, tears the bandages off, runs into the jungle smashing trees. He is not angry — he is broken.
  2. **Jinbe's Words** — Jinbe grabs him: "Stop counting what you have lost! Tell me — what do you still have?!" Harry counts on his fingers: Zoro. Nami. Usopp. Sanji. Chopper. Robin. Franky. Brook. His crew. They are alive. They are waiting for him.
  3. **Rayleigh's Proposal** — Rayleigh offers to train Harry for two years on Rusukaina. Two years away from his crew. Two years of getting stronger.
  4. **The Message: 3D2Y** — Harry returns to Marineford, rings the Ox Bell sixteen times, and shows "3D2Y" written on his arm with the 3D crossed out. The message: not three days. Two years.
  5. **Training on Rusukaina** — Rayleigh teaches Observation Haki, Armament Haki, and Conqueror's Haki control. Harry trains for two years against 500 species of powerful beasts.
  6. **Zoro Begs Mihawk** — On Kuraigana Island, Zoro bows his head to Mihawk: "Train me. Please." Mihawk agrees. The world's greatest swordsman teaches his future successor.
  7. **The Crew's Training** — Montage: Sanji learns Sky Walk and vital recipes. Nami studies weather science. Usopp becomes physically strong with Pop Greens. Chopper learns new forms. Robin trains with the Revolutionary Army. Franky discovers Vegapunk's lab. Brook becomes a rock star.
  8. **Two Years Later** — Harry stands on Rusukaina surrounded by unconscious animals. Rayleigh: "You have gotten strong." Harry puts on his straw hat and looks toward the sea.
- **Emotional Beat:** Jinbe pulling Harry back from despair — "What do you still have?" Zoro bowing to Mihawk. Every crew member training for Harry.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry counting his crew on his fingers, Zoro bowing before Mihawk, Harry standing over defeated animals
- **Crew After Chapter:** All crew members training separately. Two years pass. Time to reunite.
