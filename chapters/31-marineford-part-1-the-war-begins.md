# Chapter 31: Marineford (Part 1) - The War Begins

- **Setting:** Marineford — Marine Headquarters. The greatest war in history.
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Whitebeard, Marco, Jozu, Vista, Whitebeard Pirates, three Admirals, Sengoku, Garp, Seven Warlords, Ace, Koby
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Stage Is Set** — Three Admirals. Seven Warlords. 100,000 elite Marines. Ace chained on the execution platform. The world watches.
  2. **Whitebeard Arrives** — The ocean splits. Whitebeard's ships emerge. He slams his fist into the air and the air itself cracks. A tsunami rises. Aokiji freezes it. The war begins.
  3. **War Erupts** — Pirates vs Marines. Whitebeard's sixteen division commanders charge. The scale is beyond anything Harry has seen.
  4. **The Warlords Choose Sides** — Hancock secretly clears a path for Harry. Mihawk swings Yoru casually. Doflamingo manipulates people like puppets.
  5. **Harry Crashes into the War** — Harry's stolen warship flies over the walls and crash-lands. Harry looks across the battlefield at Ace and charges forward.
  6. **Fighting Through Hell** — Harry battles across the plaza. He fights Vice Admirals, Pacifistas, elite Marines. Every yard is bought with blood. He is running on willpower.
  7. **Ace's Backstory Revealed** — Sengoku reveals Ace is the son of Gol D. Roger. Ace grew up hating his own blood.
  8. **Oars Jr.** — A giant charges toward the execution platform to save Ace. The Warlords cut him down one by one.
  9. **Whitebeard's Wound** — Squard, manipulated by Marines, stabs Whitebeard through the chest. Whitebeard hugs him: "You are still my son."
  10. **The Wall** — Marines raise massive steel walls. Whitebeard punches through one.
  11. **Conqueror's Haki** — Harry is surrounded by thousands. Something inside him snaps. A wave of invisible force explodes outward. 50,000 Marines collapse. Everyone stares. Whitebeard grins: "That kid... has it."
  12. **Harry's Declaration** — Harry keeps running, screaming: "ACE! I'M COMING TO SAVE YOU!" Ace starts to cry.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry's Conqueror's Haki erupting for the first time. Ace's true parentage. Harry running through an impossible battlefield.
- **Anime Moments:** Whitebeard cracking the air, Harry's Conqueror's Haki knocking out 50,000 Marines, Harry running through the battlefield screaming Ace's name
