# Chapter 17: Return and the Davy Back Fight

- **Setting:** The sea, Long Ring Long Land
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Foxy (Silver Fox), Aokiji (Admiral)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Return with Gold** — The crew crashes back into the Blue Sea loaded with Skypiea gold. They exchange some at a nearby island. The crew is rich (temporarily). A relaxed, fun stretch of sailing. Crew bonding. Meals. Zoro trains. Sanji cooks. Usopp tinkers with Dials. Robin reads. Chopper studies medicine. Harry naps on the figurehead. Let Harry enjoy a peaceful moment with the crew.
  2. **Long Ring Long Land** — They arrive at a bizarre island where everything is long — long dogs, long horses, long people. The crew explores this silly place. Then they meet Foxy the Silver Fox and his crew.
  3. **The Davy Back Fight Challenge** — Foxy challenges Harry to a Davy Back Fight — an ancient pirate tradition where crews compete in games and the winner steals crew members from the loser. Harry accepts because he never backs down from a challenge (Nami is furious). The games are silly and chaotic: a boat race, a ball game, and a captain's duel.
  4. **The Games** — Foxy has the Slow-Slow Fruit — he can shoot beams that slow anything down for 30 seconds. He cheats constantly. The crew must use teamwork and creativity to win. Chopper is briefly lost to Foxy's crew (devastating). They win him back. The ball game is chaotic — Zoro and Sanji fight each other as much as the enemy. Let Harry play the games and experience the comedy.
  5. **Harry vs Foxy** — The captain's duel. Foxy's Slow-Slow beams are tricky. Harry figures out he can use a mirror to reflect the beams. He defeats Foxy with a Gum Gum Flail. The crew stays together. A fun, low-stakes arc — the last one for a while.
  6. **A Man on the Ice** — After the Davy Back Fight, the crew encounters a tall, thin man sleeping on the beach. He wears a sleep mask and a Marine uniform. He is lazy, casual, and seems harmless. Then Robin sees him and goes white. She is terrified. This is Admiral Aokiji — Kuzan. Ice-Ice Fruit. One of the three most powerful Marines in the world.
  7. **Aokiji's Warning** — Aokiji tells the crew he is "just passing through." But he singles out Robin. He knows her past: Ohara, the Buster Call, the 20-year bounty. He warns the crew: "That woman will bring catastrophe to you." Robin is shaking. Aokiji looks at Harry: "Can you really protect her from what is coming?" Let Harry respond to Aokiji's warning about Robin.
  8. **Aokiji Freezes Robin** — As a demonstration of power, Aokiji touches Robin and freezes her entire body solid — encased in ice. The crew panics. Harry is furious. He attacks Aokiji. Gum Gum Pistol — Aokiji's body turns to ice and reforms. Gum Gum Gatling — passes through. Harry cannot land a single real hit. Aokiji freezes Harry's arm, then his leg. A single Ice Time finishes it — Harry is flash-frozen.
  9. **The Power Gap** — The crew barely rescues Harry and Robin by thawing them. Aokiji let them go. He could have killed all of them. This is what an Admiral is. The crew is silent on the ship that night. They are not ready. Robin stares at the ocean, alone, thinking about what Aokiji said. Her past is catching up. Something is coming. Let Harry sit with the weight of this defeat — the first enemy he simply cannot beat.
- **Emotional Beat:** The crew's first encounter with an Admiral-level threat. The terrifying realization that they are still small fish. Robin's hidden past begins to surface — foreshadowing Water 7.
- **Anime Moments:** Aokiji freezing Robin solid, Harry's rubber fist passing through Aokiji's ice body, the crew sitting in silence after the defeat
