# Chapter 10: Little Garden - The Island of Giants

- **Setting:** Little Garden — a prehistoric island where dinosaurs still roam
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Vivi, Dorry (giant warrior of Elbaf), Brogy (giant warrior of Elbaf), Mr. 3 (Wax-Wax Fruit), Miss Goldenweek (color trap), Mr. 5, Miss Valentine
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Prehistoric Island** — The Log Pose leads the crew to Little Garden — and the moment they dock, a dinosaur walks past the ship. A T-Rex. Casually. Usopp nearly falls overboard. The island is a primordial jungle frozen in time — volcanoes, prehistoric plants, massive insects, and creatures that should have been extinct for millions of years. The air is hot and heavy. Everything is enormous. Harry is thrilled. Usopp wants to leave immediately. Let Harry explore the jungle and react to the dinosaurs.
  2. **Dorry the Giant** — Harry wanders into the jungle and discovers a massive figure sitting by a volcano, roasting a dinosaur on a spit. It's Dorry — a giant from Elbaf, nearly 70 feet tall with a Viking helmet and a chipped, ancient sword. He's cheerful, loud, and immediately offers Harry some dinosaur meat. They sit together and eat. Dorry tells Harry about Elbaf — the island of warrior giants, where honor and bravery are everything. Harry is fascinated. Let Harry eat dinosaur meat with a giant and ask about Elbaf.
  3. **Brogy the Giant** — Meanwhile, on the other side of the island, Usopp stumbles into Brogy — the other giant. Brogy is similarly enormous, with a different Viking style and a huge axe. He's just as friendly. Usopp is terrified at first but quickly becomes captivated by Brogy's stories of Elbaf warriors. Brogy treats Usopp like a fellow warrior. Usopp puffs up with pride. Something awakens in Usopp — the dream of being a brave warrior, like the giants of Elbaf.
  4. **The 100-Year Duel** — A volcano erupts — that's the signal. Dorry and Brogy stand, face each other across the island, and charge. They fight with everything they have — sword against axe, two giants clashing with force that shakes the earth. It's beautiful and terrifying. When it's over, it's a draw. Again. They've been fighting this same duel for over 100 years. Neither remembers what started it — something about a hunting contest, who caught the bigger fish. But the fight itself is sacred. It's about honor, about proving themselves to each other, about a friendship so deep it can only be expressed through combat. Usopp watches with tears in his eyes: "THIS is what warriors are." Let Harry and the crew witness the duel and react.
  5. **Sabotage** — Mr. 3 and his partner Miss Goldenweek have been hiding on the island. Mr. 3 (Wax-Wax Fruit — creates wax that hardens like steel) sabotages the duel by putting explosives in the giants' drinks. Dorry's ale explodes in his stomach during the next duel. He falls. Brogy wins, but he knows something is wrong — Dorry would never fall from a fair blow. The honor of the duel has been violated. Brogy is devastated. Mr. 3 reveals himself and traps the weakened Dorry in a wax cage.
  6. **The Wax Trap** — Mr. 3 creates his masterpiece — a giant candelabra of wax. Nami, Vivi, and Zoro are caught in it, slowly being covered in hardening wax that will turn them into statues. The wax drips and solidifies. They're being encased alive. Zoro grabs his sword and starts cutting at his own legs, trying to free himself — he'd rather lose his legs than die trapped. The others scream at him to stop. Usopp arrives and realizes he has to do something.
  7. **Usopp's Fire Star** — Usopp, shaking with fear, steps up. Mr. 3's wax is strong — but it's still wax. Wax melts. Usopp loads a Fire Star into his slingshot and shoots at the base of the candelabra. The wax catches fire. Harry arrives and smashes through the melting wax to free the crew. Mr. 3 tries to fight back, but Harry stretches his arm and delivers a Gum Gum Bazooka that sends the wax man flying. Miss Goldenweek's color traps (paint that controls emotions) nearly stop Harry — she paints "relaxation" on him and he sits down to have tea mid-battle. Usopp burns the paint off him. Let Harry deal with Miss Goldenweek's weird powers.
  8. **The Giants' Farewell** — With Mr. 3 defeated, Dorry and Brogy stand together for the first time in 100 years — as allies rather than opponents. The crew needs to leave, but a massive Sea King-infested current blocks the way. The two giants stand at the shore, raise their weapons, and deliver a combined attack — a single, devastating simultaneous slash that splits the sea and kills the Sea King. The path opens. It's the most powerful display of strength the crew has ever seen. Brogy to the crew: "Go with the pride of Elbaf warriors behind you!" Let Harry thank the giants. Usopp declares his dream: "I'm going to visit Elbaf someday and become a brave warrior of the sea!"
- **Emotional Beat:** The giants' 100-year duel — honor and friendship that transcends time. Zoro willing to cut off his own legs rather than die trapped. Usopp's dream of Elbaf is born.
- **Anime Moments:** Dorry and Brogy clashing in their 100-year duel, Zoro trying to cut his own legs free, the giants' combined attack splitting the sea
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji (+ Vivi)
