# Chapter 48: Wano (Act 2) - Kozuki Oden's Legend

- **Setting:** Wano, 20+ years ago (flashback)
- **Key Characters:** Kozuki Oden, Whitebeard, Roger, Toki, the Scabbards, Kaido, Orochi
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Young Oden** — Wild, reckless, impossibly strong. The Nine Scabbards joined him.
  2. **Sailing with Whitebeard and Roger** — Oden sailed with both crews. Met Toki. Fell in love. Roger borrowed him to read Poneglyphs.
  3. **Laugh Tale** — They found the One Piece. The crew laughed so hard they cried. Roger named it "Laugh Tale."
  4. **Return to Wano** — Oden found Kaido and Orochi had destroyed Wano. Factories, poison, slavery.
  5. **The Five-Year Shame** — Orochi's deal: dance naked weekly for five years, hostages freed each time. Oden bore the shame in silence.
  6. **Orochi's Betrayal** — Five years later, Orochi broke the deal. Oden attacked Kaido, left a permanent scar, but was defeated.
  7. **The Boiling** — Execution: boiled alive in oil. Oden held all nine Scabbards above his head while his body burned. One hour.
  8. **"I Was Born to Boil"** — Oden hurled the Scabbards to safety. **"I AM KOZUKI ODEN, AND I WAS BORN TO BOIL!"** Toki sent Scabbards twenty years forward. Oden died grinning.
- **Emotional Beat:** Oden's entire life. The shame. The boiling. "I was born to boil."
- **Anime Moments:** Roger's crew laughing at Laugh Tale, Oden dancing in shame, Oden holding Scabbards above boiling oil
