# Chapter 78: The Straw Hat

- **Setting:** The open sea — a quiet stretch of the unified ocean
- **Key Characters:** Harry, full crew, Shanks (one final visit)
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **A Quiet Day** — A rare, peaceful day. The crew goes about daily routines. Just a day in the life of the Pirate King's crew.
  2. **Harry and the Hat** — Harry really looks at the hat. Battered, patched, faded. Each patch is a memory. Each one is a scar that healed.
  3. **The Hat's History** — Worn by Joyboy, Roger, Shanks, Harry. A simple straw hat that has seen the entire history of the world.
  4. **Shanks's Visit** — The Red Force pulls alongside. Shanks comes aboard casually. They sit together on the figurehead.
  5. **The Conversation** — Shanks tells Harry how Roger cried when he found the One Piece — tears of joy for the future person. "He knew about the idea of you."
  6. **The Question** — "What does the King of the Pirates do now?" Harry: "He sails. He eats. He finds cool stuff. He helps people."
  7. **The Farewell** — Shanks: "Thank you. For being exactly who I hoped you'd be." Harry: "Thanks for the hat, old man." Shanks laughs and sails away.
  8. **Crew on Deck** — Dinner together. The table is too small. Elbows bump. Harry eats like a hurricane. Zoro and Sanji argue. This is the treasure.
  9. **Harry's Realization** — The One Piece was also this — the connections between people. The crew. The bonds. And Harry has had it all along.
  10. **Night on the Sunny** — The crew falls asleep one by one. Jinbe keeps watch. Harry is the last to close his eyes, straw hat over his face, smiling.
- **Emotional Beat:** Shanks's farewell — the father figure saying goodbye with pride. Two pirates, looking at the same sea.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry and Shanks on the figurehead, the crew at dinner, the crew asleep under the stars
