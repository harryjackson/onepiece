# Chapter 30: Impel Down (Part 2) - The Great Escape

- **Setting:** Impel Down — Levels 5 and 6
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Bon Clay, Ivankov, Jinbe, Crocodile (freed), Buggy, Magellan, Blackbeard
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Racing to Level 6** — Harry charges downward toward Level 6 — Eternal Hell. Guards cannot believe he survived Magellan's poison.
  2. **Ace Is Gone** — Harry reaches Level 6 and finds Ace's cell empty. Ace has been transferred to Marineford. Too late. The execution is tomorrow.
  3. **Unlikely Allies** — Jinbe pledges to help Harry. Crocodile is freed with blackmail material. An uneasy alliance.
  4. **The Breakout Begins** — The escape goes UP through every level. Harry leads a growing army of freed prisoners. Total chaos.
  5. **Magellan's Vengeance** — Magellan intercepts them with Venom Demon. Mr. 3 uses wax barriers and coats Harry's fists. They can only run.
  6. **The Gates of Justice** — The escapees reach the surface but the Gates of Justice are closed. Without someone inside to open them, they are trapped.
  7. **Bon Clay's Sacrifice** — Bon Clay disguises himself as Warden Magellan and walks back into Impel Down alone. He orders the gates opened. The soldiers obey. The gates swing open.
  8. **"Live Your Life Without Regrets!"** — The gates begin to close. Bon Clay: "GO, STRAW HAT! LIVE YOUR LIFE WITHOUT REGRETS!" The connection cuts. The gates slam shut. Bon Clay is trapped. He faces Magellan smiling. Harry sails toward Marineford, tears streaming.
  9. **Blackbeard Enters** — Meanwhile, Blackbeard walks into Impel Down to recruit the worst criminals for his crew.
- **Emotional Beat:** Bon Clay's sacrifice. The ultimate act of friendship from a former enemy. "Live your life without regrets."
- **Anime Moments:** Bon Clay disguised as Magellan ordering the gates open, the Gates of Justice slamming shut as Harry screams
