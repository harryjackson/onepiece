# Chapter 51: Wano (Act 3, Part 2) - The Rooftop

- **Setting:** Onigashima — the rooftop, the skull dome
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Zoro, Law, Kid, Killer vs Kaido and Big Mom
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Five vs Two** — THE ROOFTOP BATTLE. Five Supernovas vs two Emperors. The sky splits. The ground cracks.
  2. **Kaido's Dragon Form** — Kaido transforms. Breath of flame. Wind blades. The Supernovas coordinate.
  3. **Big Mom's Power** — Big Mom summons Prometheus, Zeus, and Napoleon. The rookies are overwhelmed.
  4. **Zoro's Scar** — Zoro draws Enma — Oden's legendary sword. **Ashura — Blades Drawn: Dead Man's Game.** The attack leaves a scar across Kaido's chest. Only Oden did this before.
  5. **Conqueror's Coating** — Harry realizes the strongest fighters coat attacks in Conqueror's Haki. He focuses. His fist crackles with dark energy. The Haki hits before the fist. The sky splits. Kaido: "So you can use it too!"
  6. **Separating the Emperors** — Law and Kid take on Big Mom to separate her from Kaido.
  7. **"Leave Kaido to Me"** — Harry: "Leave Kaido to me. I will beat him. Even if I die."
  8. **One on One** — Harry and Kaido clash. Conqueror's Haki collides. The sky splits again and again.
- **Emotional Beat:** Zoro scarring Kaido with Oden's sword. Harry awakening Conqueror's coating. "Leave Kaido to me."
- **Anime Moments:** Five Supernovas facing two Emperors, Zoro's attack scarring Kaido, Harry's first Conqueror's-coated punch
