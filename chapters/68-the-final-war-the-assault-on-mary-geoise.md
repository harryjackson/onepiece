# Chapter 68: The Final War - The Assault on Mary Geoise

- **Setting:** The Red Line, the seas surrounding it, the ascent to Mary Geoise
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Dragon, Sabo, Revolutionary Army, Marines, Gorosei
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **The Wall** — The alliance reaches the base of the Red Line. Dragon's forces begin the assault on the Red Port.
  2. **Marine Defense Lines** — Admiral Akainu has assembled the full might of the Marines.
  3. **Harry Climbs the Red Line** — Harry activates Gear 5 and begins climbing the Red Line itself, stretching and launching.
  4. **The Gorosei Descend** — The Five Elders in their demon forms. Each engages a different section of the alliance.
  5. **Dragon vs. Saturn** — Dragon finally reveals his full power — storms that darken the sky. "Today that ends."
  6. **Sabo vs. Marine Commanders** — Sabo wields Ace's fire techniques, perfected. Every flame is a tribute.
  7. **Koby's Stand** — Koby refuses to fire on the alliance fleet. "This isn't justice!" The Marines fracture.
  8. **The Straw Hats Reach Mary Geoise** — Harry starts ripping chains off every slave he can find. The crew follows suit.
  9. **Freeing the Slaves** — Humans, fishmen, minks, giants — all enslaved for generations. This is liberation.
  10. **The Empty Throne** — Someone is sitting in it. A figure shrouded in shadow. Imu.
  11. **Imu Revealed** — Alive for 800 years. "Every few centuries, someone like you appears. They all failed."
  12. **The True Enemy** — Imu reveals Uranus — integrated into the Red Line itself. "Come, then, 'King of the Pirates.'"
- **Emotional Beat:** Koby standing against Akainu — the cowardly boy from Chapter 1 becoming a man of true justice.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry climbing the Red Line in Gear 5, freeing slaves in Mary Geoise, Imu revealed on the Empty Throne
