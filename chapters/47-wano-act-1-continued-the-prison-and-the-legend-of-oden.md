# Chapter 47: Wano (Act 1 continued) - The Prison and the Legend of Oden

- **Setting:** Wano — the prison mines, various locations
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Kid (fellow prisoner), the crew preparing in the background, Old Man Hyogoro
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Prison Mines** — Harry works in Udon prison with seastone cuffs. Uses them as weights. Grows stronger. Kid is also imprisoned — they compete.
  2. **Old Man Hyogoro** — Hyogoro teaches Harry Ryuo — projecting Haki outward without touching. The key to hurting Kaido.
  3. **Big Mom in the Prison** — Big Mom arrives with amnesia, causing chaos. Harry stages a breakout.
  4. **Haki Breakthrough** — Fighting Queen, Harry grasps Ryuo — projecting Haki through armor.
  5. **The Rebellion Plan** — Attack Onigashima during the Fire Festival with 4,000 samurai. The crescent moon symbol spreads secretly.
  6. **Yasuie's Sacrifice** — A beloved daimyo executed by Orochi. He laughs as he dies — SMILE stole every expression but laughter. His death saves the rebellion.
  7. **Rising Tension** — Harry is free, stronger, learning Ryuo. The Fire Festival approaches.
- **Emotional Beat:** Hyogoro teaching Ryuo. Yasuie dying laughing — the cruelty of SMILE fruits.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry projecting Haki for the first time, Yasuie laughing as he dies, the crescent moon tattoos
