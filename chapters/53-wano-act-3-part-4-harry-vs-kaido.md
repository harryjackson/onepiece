# Chapter 53: Wano (Act 3, Part 4) - Harry vs Kaido

- **Setting:** Onigashima rooftop and sky above Wano
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Kaido
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Round After Round** — Harry vs Kaido continues. Kaido shifts between dragon, hybrid, and human forms. Harry uses every technique.
  2. **Kaido's Philosophy** — "Only the strong can choose how they die. Can you give me a glorious death, Straw Hat?"
  3. **Joyboy** — Kaido speaks of Joyboy. "I know you are not Joyboy either." He sounds almost disappointed.
  4. **Harry Falls** — Harry is knocked down. Gets back up. Falls. Gets up. Kaido: "Why do you keep standing?!" Harry: "Because I promised."
  5. **Yamato Holds the Line** — Yamato — Kaido's own child — fights Kaido to buy time.
  6. **Momonosuke's Courage** — Momonosuke must create flame clouds to save the Flower Capital. He is terrified but remembers Oden. He roars and carries the island.
  7. **CP0 Interference** — A CP0 agent grabs Harry and holds him still. Kaido hits Harry while restrained. Harry falls. This time he does not get up.
  8. **Harry Dies** — Harry's voice disappears from Observation Haki. Everyone feels the absence. Kaido announces: "Straw Hat Harry is dead."
  9. **Silence** — The alliance wavers. Hope dies.
  10. **The Drums** — Then — a sound. Distant. Rhythmic. The drums of liberation. Ba-dum. Ba-dum. Harry's body twitches. His hair begins to change color.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry falling and not getting up. His voice vanishing. Then — the drums. Something is waking up.
- **Anime Moments:** Kaido asking "Why do you keep standing?", Harry's voice disappearing, the first sound of the drums
