# Chapter 55: Wano (Resolution) - The Dawn of Wano

- **Setting:** Wano Country — post-battle
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Momonosuke, Kin'emon, Yamato, the people of Wano
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Wano Celebrates** — The Fire Festival transforms into a genuine celebration of freedom.
  2. **Momonosuke Becomes Shogun** — Momonosuke is declared the new Shogun. He is terrified but stands tall.
  3. **The Crew Recovers** — Rest and healing. Crew bonding across the country.
  4. **Yamato's Choice** — Yamato wants to join the crew but chooses to stay and protect Wano first.
  5. **Green Bull Attacks** — Admiral Ryokugyu arrives. Before the crew can fight, Shanks releases Conqueror's Haki from offshore. Green Bull retreats. Shanks does not even set foot on the island.
  6. **Pluton** — Robin reveals Pluton — one of the three Ancient Weapons — is hidden beneath Wano.
  7. **Farewell** — The Straw Hats say goodbye to Wano. Tama waves. Momonosuke tries not to cry (he fails).
- **Emotional Beat:** Momonosuke becoming Shogun. Shanks's Haki warning from offshore. Farewell to Wano.
- **Anime Moments:** Momonosuke declared Shogun, Shanks's Conqueror's Haki repelling Green Bull, the Sunny sailing away
- **Crew After Chapter:** Full crew. Emperor status.
