# Chapter 54: Wano (Act 3, Part 5) - Gear 5: The Warrior of Liberation

- **Setting:** Onigashima, the sky above Wano
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Kaido, Momonosuke, Zunesha
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Awakening** — Harry's Devil Fruit AWAKENS. It was never the Gum-Gum Fruit. It is the Human-Human Fruit, Model: Nika — a Mythical Zoan. The Sun God Nika, the Warrior of Liberation.
  2. **GEAR 5** — Harry's hair turns white. His clothes turn white. His eyes glow. His heartbeat sounds like drums. He is laughing. Zunesha weeps: "Joyboy... has returned."
  3. **Cartoon Physics** — Harry grabs lightning and throws it back. He runs through the air like a cartoon character. He turns the ground to rubber. He inflates Kaido from the inside. The fight is simultaneously the funniest and most terrifying thing anyone has ever seen.
  4. **Kaido's Disbelief** — Kaido fights back with everything. Harry dodges by flattening like paper, stretching like taffy, bouncing like a rubber ball.
  5. **The Limits of Gear 5** — Gear 5 runs out temporarily — Harry ages rapidly for a moment. The drums return. As long as Harry is having fun, he cannot be beaten.
  6. **Momonosuke's Flame Clouds** — An 8-year-old boy holding up a nation on his shoulders — just like his father held up the Scabbards.
  7. **Kaido's Final Form** — Kaido coats himself in magma. A dragon made of fire and rock.
  8. **Bajrang Gun** — Harry's fist becomes the size of Onigashima itself. Coated in Conqueror's Haki, Advanced Armament, and Gear 5 power. **"GUM GUM — BAJRANG GUN."** The fist punches through Kaido, through the island, driving him into the magma chamber beneath Wano.
  9. **Dawn** — Kaido falls. The sky clears. For the first time in twenty years, the sun rises over Wano. Dawn.
  10. **Emperor** — Harry's new bounty: 3 BILLION berries. He is declared one of the new Four Emperors of the Sea.
- **Emotional Beat:** Gear 5 awakening — joy, liberation, laughter. Zunesha crying "Joyboy has returned." Dawn breaking over Wano. Harry becoming an Emperor.
- **Anime Moments:** Gear 5 Harry — white hair, laughing, drums of liberation; Bajrang Gun descending; Dawn breaking over Wano
- **Crew After Chapter:** Full crew victorious. Harry is an Emperor. Wano is free.
