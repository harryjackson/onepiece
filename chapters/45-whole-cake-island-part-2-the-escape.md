# Chapter 45: Whole Cake Island (Part 2) - The Escape

- **Setting:** Whole Cake Island and the seas of Totto Land
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Sanji, the crew, Katakuri, Big Mom, Bege, Pudding, Germa 66
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Wedding Crashes** — Harry bursts from inside the wedding cake. Big Mom shrieks with Conqueror's Haki.
  2. **Big Mom's Power** — She is beyond anything they can handle. The plan to assassinate her fails. They cannot kill Big Mom.
  3. **Sanji Returns** — Sanji saves his family despite everything they did to him. He rejects his Vinsmoke name. He returns to Harry. He prepares a meal. "I am back, Captain." Harry eats and cries: "Your food is so good!"
  4. **The Escape Begins** — The crew flees on the Sunny. Big Mom pursues with her entire fleet.
  5. **Harry vs Katakuri: The Mirror World** — Harry enters the Mirror World to fight Charlotte Katakuri — Big Mom's strongest son. Advanced Observation Haki — he can see the future. He has never lost.
  6. **The Longest Fight** — The battle stretches for hours. Harry begins to see the future too. Observation Haki evolving in real time.
  7. **Katakuri's Honor** — When Flampe interferes, Katakuri stabs himself to even the playing field. He respects Harry.
  8. **Gear 4: Snakeman** — Harry activates Snakeman. Attacks stretch, curve, and accelerate — each punch changes direction. "Gum Gum — Black Mamba."
  9. **King Cobra** — The final exchange. Both fighters fall. Katakuri falls on his back for the first time in his life. He asks Harry: "Will you come back someday and defeat Big Mom?" Harry: "I AM GOING TO BEAT EVERYONE."
  10. **Sanji's Cake** — Sanji bakes the most incredible cake. Big Mom eats it and the hunger rampage ends. Sanji's cooking saves the crew without a single punch.
  11. **The Escape** — Jinbe takes the helm and holds back the Big Mom fleet to let the Sunny escape.
  12. **New Bounty** — Harry's new bounty: 1.5 BILLION berries. The newspapers call him "The Fifth Emperor."
- **Emotional Beat:** Sanji returning and cooking for Harry. Katakuri's respect — the duel of warriors. Harry declared the Fifth Emperor.
- **Anime Moments:** Gear 4 Snakeman, Katakuri falling on his back, Harry's 1.5 billion bounty poster
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry's group reunited with Sanji. Jinbe staying behind. Pedro dead. Heading for Wano. Bounty: 1.5 billion.
