# Chapter 67: The Final War Begins - Gathering the Storm

- **Setting:** The seas around the Red Line, various locations worldwide
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Dragon, Sabo, Revolutionary Army, Vivi, allies from every arc
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **The World Mobilizes** — Allies respond worldwide: Vivi, Momonosuke, Shirahoshi, Sabo, every Grand Fleet captain.
  2. **Father and Son** — Harry meets Dragon. "You kind of look like a villain." Dragon almost smiles.
  3. **Sabo and Harry Reunite** — Sabo tells Harry about Imu: "Not human. Not anymore."
  4. **The Alliance Forms** — The largest pirate-and-rebel alliance in history. Harry lays out the plan: "We punch our way to the top."
  5. **Vivi Returns** — Vivi tells them about Imu's power to erase islands from the sky.
  6. **Robin's Briefing** — The Red Line must be destroyed at a specific point — directly above Fishman Island.
  7. **The Marine Split** — Fujitora and Koby refuse to fight the alliance. The Marines are fracturing.
  8. **Aokiji's Choice** — Aokiji offers crucial information about Imu. "I froze my heart once. Not again."
  9. **Harry's Speech** — "I don't want to be your boss. I just want everyone to be free. Also, there's gonna be a huge party when we win."
  10. **The Straw Hat Grand Fleet Assembles** — Over five thousand warriors. Harry didn't ask for followers. He just made friends.
  11. **The Eve of Battle** — Quiet moments. Zoro meditates. Usopp writes to Kaya. Each crew member prepares.
  12. **Dawn of the Final Battle** — The fleet surges forward. The final war begins.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry meeting Dragon for the first time. The awkward, understated reunion.
- **Anime Moments:** The war council, the Grand Fleet assembling, dawn breaking over the Red Line
