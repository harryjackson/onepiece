# Chapter 65: Laugh Tale - The One Piece (Part 1)

- **Setting:** The heart of Laugh Tale — the Poneglyph chamber and the treasure vault beyond
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Robin, crew, Blackbeard (defeated temporarily), Joyboy's legacy
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **Robin Completes the Translation** — The Ancient Kingdom united all races. Joyboy's plan: destroy the Red Line, merge all oceans into one (the "All Blue"), raise the sunken lands. The Twenty Kings destroyed the Ancient Kingdom before it could happen.
  2. **The Broken Promise** — Joyboy made promises to every race. He failed. He left everything on Laugh Tale for the person who would come after him. The "One Piece" is his legacy.
  3. **The Vault Opens** — Beyond the Poneglyph chamber, a massive vault responds to the Drums of Liberation.
  4. **The One Piece** — The Rio Poneglyph, ancient blueprints, and a letter from Joyboy. The plan: use all three Ancient Weapons together to break the Red Line and unite the world. The "One Piece" — making the world one piece.
  5. **Harry Laughs** — Harry starts laughing. Deep, joyful, unstoppable. It's exactly what he's always wanted. Not treasure. Not power. A free world. Roger laughed for the same reason.
  6. **The Crew Reacts** — Sanji drops to his knees — the All Blue is what happens when the Red Line falls. Nami realizes her map must be redrawn. Robin weeps. Chopper understands medicine shared freely. Franky cries giant manly tears.
  7. **Zoro's Quiet Moment** — Zoro leans against the wall and nods once at Harry. "I chose the right captain."
  8. **Brook and the Music** — Brook finds an ancient song from 800 years ago. It sounds like Binks' Sake. He plays with his whole soul.
  9. **Jinbe's Pledge** — "Let's keep Joyboy's promise. Let's bring Fishman Island to the sun."
  10. **Usopp the Witness** — Usopp realizes his role: the storyteller. "I, the Great Captain Usopp, was THERE when the One Piece was found!" For once, it's not a lie.
  11. **The Ancient Weapons Blueprint** — Detailed instructions for using all three weapons in concert. But the World Government has Uranus. Taking it back will require war.
  12. **Leaving the Vault** — Harry addresses his crew: "Do we do it? Do we break the Red Line?" Every single crew member answers yes. Harry grins: "Then let's go start a war."
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry laughing. The same laugh Roger gave. The One Piece is the dream of a free, united world.
- **Anime Moments:** The vault opening in ethereal light, Harry laughing like Roger, Brook playing the ancient song
