# Chapter 19: Water 7 (Part 2) - Harry vs Usopp

- **Setting:** Water 7, the shore by the Going Merry
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Usopp
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Duel** — Night. The shore by the Going Merry. Harry and Usopp face each other. This is not a game. Usopp has prepared — impact dials, smoke stars, caltrops, rotten eggs, pepper bombs. He knows he cannot win in a straight fight. He has to be clever. And he is. Usopp fights with everything — every trick, every trap, every ounce of cunning. He hits Harry with impact dials that send shockwaves through his rubber body. He blinds him with smoke. He trips him with caltrops. Let Harry fight Usopp — and feel the pain of fighting a friend.
  2. **Usopp's Last Stand** — Usopp is outmatched in raw power. Harry is stronger, faster, tougher. But Usopp refuses to stay down. He gets up, bleeding, broken, and fires again. And again. He screams about the Merry, about being weak, about being left behind. "I know I'm not strong! I know I can't keep up! But I can't just let you throw her away!" The crew watches from the shadows. Nami is crying. Sanji clenches his fists. Chopper sobs. Zoro stands rigid.
  3. **Harry Ends It** — Harry hits Usopp with a Gum Gum Bullet — one clean hit that ends the fight. Usopp crumples. Harry stands over him, tears streaming down his face. He wants to say sorry. He wants to take it back. But he is the captain. A captain cannot be swayed on a decision that affects the crew's survival. Harry walks away. "The Going Merry is yours. Take care of her." Usopp lies on the shore. He is no longer a Straw Hat.
  4. **The Crew's Darkest Night** — The crew returns to their lodging in silence. Nobody eats. Nobody speaks. Harry sits alone, hat over his eyes. The crew is broken. The family has fractured. This is worse than any villain, any battle. Let Harry sit with this pain.
  5. **Iceburg Is Shot** — That same night, Iceburg is shot in his office. The suspects: Nico Robin and a group of masked figures. The city turns on the crew. Robin is seen with the attackers — CP9, though nobody knows their identity yet.
  6. **Robin's Goodbye** — Harry finds Robin on the bridge. She looks at him with cold, empty eyes: "Do not follow me. Forget about me. Goodbye." She walks away into the rain. Harry shouts after her. She does not turn around. Something is very wrong — Robin would not do this willingly. But why? Let Harry decide what to believe — Robin's words or his instincts.
  7. **CP9 — The Hidden Assassins** — The truth emerges: CP9 is a secret World Government assassination unit. Four of their agents have been living undercover in Water 7 for FIVE YEARS — disguised as Galley-La shipwrights. They want Robin because she can read Poneglyphs. They have threatened to call a Buster Call (total military annihilation) on the Straw Hats if Robin does not surrender. Robin is sacrificing herself to save her crew.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry vs Usopp — the crew's darkest moment. The captain and his friend tearing each other apart. Robin's silent sacrifice. The crew breaking apart.
- **Anime Moments:** Usopp firing impact dials at Harry while crying, Harry walking away after winning, Robin saying goodbye in the rain
