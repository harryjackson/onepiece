# Chapter 46: Wano (Act 1) - The Land of Samurai

- **Setting:** Wano Country — a feudal Japan-inspired isolated nation
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Zoro, crew, Kin'emon, Momonosuke, O-Tama, Tsuru, the people of Wano
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arriving in Wano** — Cherry blossoms, pagodas, mountains. But rivers are purple with poison. People starve. Wano has been under Kaido and Shogun Orochi for 20 years.
  2. **O-Tama** — Harry meets a starving girl saving rice for when Ace returns. Harry tells her Ace is dead. She does not cry. Harry gives her his food.
  3. **Zoro in the Capital** — Zoro sees a man executed for fishing. He picks up a cursed blade from a shrine.
  4. **Holdem** — Kaido's subordinate kidnaps Tama. Harry smashes him with Red Hawk.
  5. **The Scabbards** — Harry meets Oden's Nine Red Scabbards — samurai who traveled through time.
  6. **Crew Undercover** — Zoro as ronin, Usopp as merchant, Robin as geisha, Franky as carpenter — each gathering allies.
  7. **Feeding the Starving** — Harry distributes stolen food to Okobore Town. Tama eats until full for the first time.
  8. **Kaido's Dragon** — A massive blue dragon fills the sky — Kaido, drunk, breathing fire.
  9. **Thunder Bagua** — Harry attacks with Gear 4. Kong Gun — Kaido barely flinches. One club swing: Harry unconscious. Captured. Prison mines.
  10. **The Gap** — Harry wakes in seastone cuffs. The gap between him and an Emperor is a chasm.
- **Emotional Beat:** Tama's hunger and hope. Harry seeing Kaido's dragon form. Harry being one-shotted.
- **Anime Moments:** Kaido's dragon form, Thunder Bagua, Tama eating until full
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry captured in prison. Crew undercover in Wano.
