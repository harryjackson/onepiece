# Chapter 41: Dressrosa (Part 2) - Birdcage

- **Setting:** Dressrosa
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Law, Doflamingo, the Donquixote Family officers, the gladiators, the dwarves
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Birdcage** — Doflamingo's ultimate technique: an enormous dome of razor strings descends over the entire island. It will slowly contract, cutting through everything. The island is a death trap with a timer.
  2. **Doflamingo's Bounty Game** — Doflamingo offers three billion berries for Harry's head. Citizens turn on the crew.
  3. **Law vs Doflamingo** — Law confronts Doflamingo. The fight is brutal. Doflamingo shoots Law multiple times and beats him to the ground.
  4. **Corazon's Story** — Corazon — Doflamingo's younger brother, a secret Marine agent — stole the Op-Op Fruit to save young Law, at the cost of his life. Corazon died smiling so Law could live.
  5. **Usopp's Impossible Shot** — Sugar is about to turn Harry and Law into toys. Usopp fires a Pop Green across an entire plateau, using Observation Haki for the first time — awakening it unconsciously. Sugar falls. The spell breaks.
  6. **The Toys Remember** — Every toy transforms back. Soldiers become men. Fathers appear from nowhere. Children run to parents they forgot. The entire country erupts in shock, tears, and fury.
  7. **The Alliance Rallies** — Gladiators, dwarves, pirates, and citizens rally together. Individual battles begin across the city. Franky vs Senor Pink. Robin vs Diamante.
  8. **Zoro vs Pica** — Pica merges with Dressrosa's bedrock and becomes a stone golem the size of a mountain. Zoro cuts the stone giant in half with a single slash visible across the island.
- **Emotional Beat:** Corazon dying with a smile for Law. The toys transforming back. Usopp's impossible shot. The city turning against its tyrant.
- **Anime Moments:** Birdcage descending, Corazon muffling Law's cries, every toy transforming back simultaneously
