# Chapter 35: Return to Sabaody - The Crew Reunites

- **Setting:** Sabaody Archipelago, two years later
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew (all reunited), Rayleigh, fake Straw Hats, Sentomaru, Pacifistas
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arriving at Sabaody** — Harry arrives two years older, taller, stronger, with a scar on his chest from the war.
  2. **The Fake Straw Hats** — A fake crew has appeared, impersonating them. The comedy escalates as the fakes try to recruit Harry into their own fake crew.
  3. **Reunions, One by One** — Each real crew member arrives. Zoro got on the wrong ship. Nami is gorgeous and confident. Usopp is muscular. Sanji's eyebrow now shows on the opposite side. Chopper has new forms. Robin is serene. Franky is MASSIVE. Brook is a celebrity. Each reunion is emotional, funny, and chaotic.
  4. **The Power Difference** — A Pacifista appears. Two years ago, it took the entire crew to barely defeat one. Harry destroys it with a single Haki-coated punch. Zoro and Sanji each destroy one with equal ease. They are monsters now.
  5. **Rayleigh's Farewell** — Rayleigh sees them off. The Thousand Sunny is coated in resin. "Roger would have liked you." The ship sinks beneath the waves.
  6. **The Deep Sea** — The Sunny sails through the deep ocean. The crew gathers on deck, talking, laughing, eating. For the first time since the crew was scattered, everything feels right.
- **Emotional Beat:** The reunion. Two years apart. Everyone has grown. They are back — and stronger than ever.
- **Anime Moments:** Each crew member arriving at Sabaody with new looks, Harry one-shotting the Pacifista, the Sunny diving toward Fishman Island
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook — full crew reunited
