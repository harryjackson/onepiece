# Chapter 3: The Clown Pirate - Buggy the Star

- **Setting:** Orange Town, East Blue
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Buggy the Clown, Mohji (beast tamer), Richie (lion), Cabaji (acrobat), Chouchou (the dog), Mayor Boodle
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Deserted Town** — Harry and Zoro arrive at Orange Town. The streets are empty. Shutters are closed. Food is still on tables — people left in a hurry. Cannonballs have smashed through buildings. In the distance, up on a rooftop, they can hear maniacal laughter and circus music. Zoro is already lost — he wandered two streets in the wrong direction and had to be dragged back. Let Harry explore the abandoned town and look for clues.
  2. **The Thief in the Alley** — Harry spots a girl sneaking through the streets with a bag of treasure. It's Nami — orange hair, clever eyes, a thief who specializes in stealing from pirates. She's drawn the most beautiful map Harry has ever seen. She's sharp, fast-talking, and immediately tries to manipulate Harry. She offers a deal: "I'll be your navigator if you help me steal Buggy's treasure." Harry agrees instantly (he's simple). Zoro is suspicious. Let Harry talk to Nami — she's charming but clearly hiding something.
  3. **Nami's Trick** — Nami ties Harry up and presents him to Buggy as a "gift" to gain Buggy's trust. Buggy is thrilled — a rubber boy! Buggy locks Harry in a cage and shows off his Chop-Chop Fruit powers: he splits his body into floating pieces, his hand flies across the room, his head detaches and cackles. He fires a Buggy Ball (a special cannonball) that destroys an entire row of buildings. Nami pretends to be loyal to Buggy while secretly pocketing his treasure map.
  4. **Zoro Crashes the Party** — Zoro shows up looking for Harry (after getting lost three more times). He cuts through Buggy's crew to reach the cage. Buggy sends Cabaji — his acrobat swordsman — to fight Zoro. Zoro is still injured from a wound he got earlier (a slash across his chest from a fight). He fights Cabaji one-handed, bleeding, refusing to give up. Let Harry cheer Zoro on from inside the cage. Zoro wins by willpower alone.
  5. **Chouchou and the Pet Food Shop** — While the fighting happens, Harry (still in the cage) witnesses something heartbreaking nearby. Chouchou — a small white dog — sits in front of a pet food shop, day after day. The old mayor, Boodle, explains: the shop belonged to Chouchou's owner, who died of illness. Chouchou guards the shop because it's all he has left of his master. It's the old man's treasure. Then Mohji (Buggy's beast tamer) and his giant lion Richie attack the shop to steal the pet food. Chouchou fights the lion — a tiny dog against a lion — and gets thrown aside, bitten, hurt. The shop burns down.
  6. **Harry's Rage** — Harry breaks out of the cage (Nami frees him or he stretches his way out). He sees Chouchou sitting in front of the burned shop, wounded and whimpering. Harry picks up the one box of pet food that survived the fire and gives it to Chouchou. Then he turns toward Mohji and Richie with fury in his eyes. He doesn't care about pirate treasure right now. He cares about a dog whose treasure was destroyed. Harry smashes Mohji and Richie with a single Gum Gum Pistol. Let Harry say something to Chouchou — a promise, a comfort, anything.
  7. **Buggy's Secret** — During the fight, Buggy reveals something shocking: he and Shanks were cabin boys together on the same pirate ship as kids. Not just any ship — Gol D. Roger's ship. Buggy HATES Shanks because Shanks accidentally made him eat the Chop-Chop Fruit, costing him the ability to swim and ruining his plan to steal a treasure map. Buggy sees Harry's straw hat and goes berserk — "THAT'S SHANKS'S HAT!" Let Harry react to learning Shanks sailed with the King of the Pirates.
  8. **Devil Fruit vs Devil Fruit** — Harry fights Buggy — the first Devil Fruit vs Devil Fruit battle. Punches don't work because Buggy splits apart. Buggy's flying knives and detached fists are unpredictable. Harry has to get creative. He figures out that if he holds Buggy's body parts, Buggy can't reassemble. Nami ties up Buggy's scattered pieces while Harry delivers the final blow — Gum Gum Bazooka sends Buggy flying into the horizon. First creative use of rubber powers in combat.
  9. **The Mayor's Tears** — After the battle, old Mayor Boodle breaks down. He's been trying to protect this town for years. He tried to fight Buggy alone and almost died. Harry tells him the town is safe now. The townspeople come out of hiding and cheer. Nami takes Buggy's treasure (of course). She agrees to travel with Harry as his navigator — but makes it clear she's NOT joining his crew. She has her own reasons for needing money. Lots of money. She won't say why.
  10. **Setting Sail** — Harry, Zoro, and Nami sail away from Orange Town. Nami is already bossing them around about navigation. Zoro is napping. Harry is sitting on the prow, staring at the sea, wearing his straw hat. He's thinking about Shanks, about Roger, about the Grand Line. Nami draws a map of their route. Let Harry ask Nami about her dream — she deflects. Something is weighing on her.
- **Emotional Beat:** Chouchou's loyalty to his dead master's shop. Harry fighting not for himself but for a dog's treasure. The reveal that Shanks sailed with Roger.
- **Anime Moments:** Buggy splitting apart for the first time, Chouchou sitting in front of the burning shop, Harry delivering the Gum Gum Bazooka
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami (temporary)
