# Chapter 7: Nami's Secret - The Arlong Park Saga (Part 2)

- **Setting:** Arlong Park, East Blue
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Zoro, Usopp, Sanji, Nami, Arlong, Hachi (six-sword octopus fishman), Kuroobi (fishman karate), Chew (water-spitting fishman), the villagers, Genzo, Nojiko
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Walk** — Harry, Zoro, Sanji, and Usopp walk toward Arlong Park. The villagers line the road, watching. Some are crying. Some are holding weapons — they were about to march on Arlong Park themselves, a suicide mission. Genzo (the sheriff with the pinwheel hat) steps forward to stop the crew: "This isn't your fight." Harry walks past him without stopping. Genzo watches the four of them go and puts his weapon down. Someone else is fighting for them now. The gates of Arlong Park loom ahead — massive stone, fishman banners, the smell of saltwater. Let Harry approach the gate. What does he feel? What does he say?
  2. **Kicking Down the Door** — Harry doesn't knock. He doesn't sneak in. He stretches his leg back and kicks the gate clean off its hinges. It crashes into the courtyard. Arlong and his fishmen are lounging around a pool. Arlong looks up, amused. "You again? The rubber boy?" Harry's response is simple and furious. He's not here for treasure. He's not here for glory. He's here because Nami asked for help. Arlong laughs: "Nami is MINE. She's been mine since she was eight years old. She'll draw maps for me until the day she dies." Let Harry respond to Arlong's claim over Nami.
  3. **Usopp vs Chew** — The fights split up. Usopp draws Chew — a fishman who can spit water like bullets. Usopp's first instinct is to run. He literally runs away, hiding behind trees, diving into bushes. Chew chases him, blasting water bullets that punch through stone. Usopp is terrified. His legs are shaking. He's crying and running and saying "I want to go home!" But then he stops. He thinks about Nami. He thinks about his own cowardice. He turns around. He's still crying, but he plants his feet: "If I run away now, I can never face anyone again!" He fights Chew with everything — slingshot, Tabasco Star (hot sauce in the eyes), a flaming alcohol attack. He wins ugly, messy, and barely conscious. But he wins. Let Harry hear about Usopp's fight afterward and react with pride.
  4. **Sanji vs Kuroobi** — Sanji takes on Kuroobi — a fishman karate master. Kuroobi drags Sanji underwater, where fishmen have a massive advantage. Sanji can't breathe, can't kick properly, and Kuroobi is in his element. Sanji is getting crushed. But Sanji is a cook — he has incredible lung capacity from years of working over hot stoves and holding his breath in smoky kitchens. He claws his way back to the surface. On land, Sanji's kicks are devastating. He unleashes a flurry — Mutton Shot, Anti-Manner Kick Course — and sends Kuroobi crashing through the wall of Arlong Park. Sanji lights a cigarette (or pretends to) and adjusts his tie. "Don't mess with a cook."
  5. **Zoro vs Hachi** — Zoro fights Hatchan (Hachi) — a six-armed octopus fishman who uses six swords simultaneously. Zoro is still carrying the massive wound from Mihawk across his chest. It hasn't healed. Every swing opens it further. Blood soaks his haramaki. Hachi has six swords; Zoro has three. But Zoro's three-sword style is on another level. Even wounded, even bleeding out, Zoro cuts through all six of Hachi's swords and drops him with one devastating slash. Zoro falls to one knee afterward. He's running on willpower alone.
  6. **Harry Reaches Arlong** — The fishmen are down. Only Arlong remains. He stands and reveals his true power — Shark On Darts. He launches himself nose-first like a torpedo, drilling through stone pillars. His teeth are weapons — he can bite through steel. He pulls his teeth out and they grow back instantly, rows of shark teeth, and he uses them like handheld blades. Arlong is faster and stronger than anything Harry has faced. He grabs Harry by the legs and swings him through the walls of his own fortress. Harry crashes through room after room.
  7. **The Map Room** — Harry crashes through a wall and lands in a small room at the top of Arlong Park. He looks around. There's a tiny desk. Stacks of maps — hundreds of them, drawn in a child's handwriting that slowly becomes an adult's. Ink stains everywhere. Quills worn down to stubs. And on the desk, on the walls, on the floor — bloodstains. Old ones. Small ones. This is where Nami was forced to draw maps from the age of eight. This is the room where her childhood was stolen. Harry sees it all — the tiny chair, the barred window, the pen she was chained to. Arlong appears in the doorway, grinning: "Impressive, isn't it? The maps she drew for me are works of art. She's the best navigator in the world — and she belongs to ME."
  8. **Harry Destroys the Room** — Something snaps in Harry. He doesn't say a word. He starts destroying the room. He grabs the desk and smashes it. He tears the maps from the walls. He punches through the shelves. He breaks the quills, shatters the ink bottles, tears the room to pieces with his bare hands. Arlong attacks — Harry doesn't stop. He takes the hits and keeps destroying. Every map. Every pen. Every chain. Every piece of Nami's prison. Arlong screams: "WHAT ARE YOU DOING?! THOSE MAPS ARE PRICELESS!" Harry, with blood on his face and fury in his eyes: "THESE MAPS WERE DRAWN WITH NAMI'S TEARS! THIS ROOM IS WHERE YOU KEPT HER TRAPPED! I'M GOING TO DESTROY ALL OF IT!" Let Harry destroy the map room. Let him feel the rage.
  9. **The Final Blow** — Arlong is furious now. Full shark rage. He charges Harry with everything — Shark On Darts, teeth attacks, his raw fishman strength. They fight through the collapsing upper floors of Arlong Park. Harry stretches his leg up through the ceiling, through the roof, high into the sky. His foot reaches above the clouds. Then it comes down — Gum Gum Battle Axe — his leg crashes through every floor of Arlong Park, through Arlong himself, through the map room, through the foundation. The entire building collapses. Arlong Park crumbles into rubble. When the dust settles, Harry stands on the ruins. Arlong is buried in the wreckage, defeated.
  10. **"Nami is my friend!"** — Harry stands on the ruins of Arlong Park and shouts to the sky: "NAMI! YOU'RE MY FRIEND!" (or let Harry declare it in his own words). Nami, standing at the edge with the crew and villagers, hears him. She clutches the straw hat on her head. Tears stream down her face. But this time, she's smiling. For the first time in eight years — real, genuine, unguarded happiness. The villagers erupt in cheers. Genzo's pinwheel spins in the wind. Nojiko hugs her sister. The nightmare is over.
  11. **The Village Celebrates** — The biggest party the Conomi Islands have ever seen. Food, music, dancing, crying, laughter. The villagers who suffered for a decade under Arlong are free. Sanji cooks a feast. Zoro drinks. Usopp tells wildly exaggerated versions of his fight. Harry eats everything in sight. Nami sits quietly for a moment, looking at Bellemere's tangerine grove in the moonlight. She touches the tangerines. "I'm free, Bellemere." Let Harry find Nami at the grove and share a quiet moment with her.
  12. **The New Tattoo** — Nami visits Nojiko's tattoo artist and gets a new tattoo over the Arlong mark on her shoulder — a pinwheel (for Genzo, who wore one to make baby Nami laugh) wrapped in tangerine vines (for Bellemere's grove). It's beautiful. It covers the scar completely. She's no longer marked by Arlong. She's marked by the people who loved her.
  13. **Nami Officially Joins** — The crew prepares to leave. Nami walks to the Going Merry (which she stole, and which is now back). The villagers come to say goodbye. Genzo grabs Harry by the collar and threatens him: "If you ever make Nami cry, I'll hunt you down." Harry just grins. Nami runs through the crowd toward the ship without stopping — as she passes the villagers, wallets fly out of their pockets and into her hands. She stole from everyone one last time, as a goodbye. She leaps onto the ship, grinning, holding fistfuls of cash: "LET'S GO!" Nami is officially part of the crew. For real this time.
  14. **The First Bounty** — As the crew sails away, the news hits: "Straw Hat Harry" has been given a bounty. 30 million berries — the highest first bounty in East Blue history. A wanted poster with Harry's grinning face. The Marines are watching now. The world is starting to notice. Nami pins the poster to the cabin wall. Harry is delighted: "I'm wanted! Cool!" Zoro: "It's not cool, idiot." Sanji: "Why isn't MY face on a poster?!" Usopp: "My bounty would be at least 100 million!" (It wouldn't.) Let Harry react to his first bounty and what it means — he's officially a pirate in the eyes of the world.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry destroying the map room. "NAMI! YOU'RE MY FRIEND!" Nami's freedom. The village celebration. This is THE defining moment of East Blue — the moment the crew becomes a family.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry kicking down the gates of Arlong Park, Harry destroying the map room while screaming about Nami's tears, Arlong Park collapsing after the Gum Gum Battle Axe
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami (official), Usopp, Sanji
