# Chapter 63: Elbaf (Resolution) - Setting Sail for the End

- **Setting:** Elbaf harbor, then the open sea heading toward Laugh Tale
- **Key Characters:** Harry, full crew, Dorry, Brogy, Bonney, the giants of Elbaf
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Morning of Departure** — Harry and Zoro sit in comfortable silence on the figurehead. "Today's the day, Zoro." Zoro smirks: "About time."
  2. **Giant's Farewell** — Hundreds of giants stomping their feet in unison — a thunderous war send-off.
  3. **Bonney's Goodbye** — Bonney watches from shore. "Come back and fix this broken world, Harry."
  4. **Crew Meeting — The Plan** — Nami lays out the route: three extreme zones — Magnetic Dead Zone, Storm Eternal, Final Current.
  5. **Rivals on the Horizon** — Multiple crews heading the same direction. Blackbeard's fleet. The race.
  6. **Sanji's Last Supper** — Sanji cooks the greatest meal of his life using ingredients from every island they've visited.
  7. **The Magnetic Dead Zone** — All instruments spin uselessly. Nami navigates by stars and weather sense alone.
  8. **The Storm Eternal** — A permanent hurricane. The crew works together at peak performance.
  9. **The Final Current** — A perfectly calm, mirror-flat ocean flowing toward Laugh Tale.
  10. **Harry's Dream** — Harry dreams of Joyboy: "I've been waiting 800 years for you. Don't keep me waiting any longer."
  11. **The Island Appears** — Usopp: "ISLAND!" Through the morning mist, an island emerges. Small, green, unremarkable. This is Laugh Tale.
  12. **The Threshold** — Harry: "We made it. Let's go see what made the King of the Pirates laugh."
- **Emotional Beat:** The island appearing through the mist — the moment the entire journey has been building toward.
- **Anime Moments:** The giants stomping in unison, Sanji's farewell meal under the stars, Laugh Tale appearing through the mist
