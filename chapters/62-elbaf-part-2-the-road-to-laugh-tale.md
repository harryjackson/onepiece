# Chapter 62: Elbaf (Part 2) - The Road to Laugh Tale

- **Setting:** Elbaf — the roots of Yggdrasil, the Sun Gate, hidden chambers
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Robin, Dorry, Brogy, Loki, Blackbeard Pirates (approaching), Nami, Usopp
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Into the Roots** — Robin leads the crew into the labyrinthine root system beneath Yggdrasil. Harry immediately gets lost.
  2. **The Murals of History** — Murals from the Void Century depicting the Ancient Kingdom and Joyboy — a figure with a straw hat, stretching and laughing. Harry stares: "That's... is that me?"
  3. **The Sun Gate** — Harry activates Gear 5. The Drums of Liberation resonate with the gate. The final Road Poneglyph sits within.
  4. **Robin Reads the Final Road Poneglyph** — Combined with the other three, this completes the map. Robin, tears in her eyes: "I found it. I can see where Laugh Tale is."
  5. **The Weight of Knowledge** — The Poneglyphs also reveal the World Government's "Great Cleansing" plan. Finding the One Piece isn't just treasure — it's about stopping them.
  6. **Blackbeard's Scouts** — Scouts from Blackbeard's crew infiltrate Elbaf. A brief skirmish erupts. The race to Laugh Tale has begun.
  7. **Loki's Gambit** — Loki attempts to break free during the chaos.
  8. **Nami Charts the Course** — Nami charts the route to Laugh Tale through impossible ocean conditions. "I'm the navigator who will map the entire world."
  9. **Usopp's Moment of Truth** — A prophecy: "A small warrior with a long nose will stand at the gates of the end." Chopper: "You always run at first. But you always come back. That's what makes you brave."
  10. **Preparing the Sunny** — Franky builds a commemorative plaque: "The Ship That Carried the King of the Pirates."
  11. **Letters Home** — Each crew member writes a message to someone they love. Harry doesn't write anything: "I'll tell everyone in person."
  12. **Departure Eve** — Dorry and Brogy present Harry with a giant battle horn. Bonney stays on Elbaf with Kuma. "Come back alive, you idiot."
- **Emotional Beat:** Robin finding the final Road Poneglyph and weeping — every scholar who died for the truth is vindicated.
- **Anime Moments:** The Sun Gate opening, Robin reading the final Road Poneglyph, the crew writing their letters home
