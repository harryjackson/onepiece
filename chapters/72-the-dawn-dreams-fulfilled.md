# Chapter 72: The Dawn - Dreams Fulfilled

- **Setting:** Various locations in the newly united world
- **Key Characters:** Harry, full crew, allies from every arc
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **The World Government Dissolves** — The Celestial Dragons are stripped of divine status. Marines reform under Koby and Fujitora.
  2. **Chopper's Discovery** — A universal vaccine framework from Vegapunk's data. "Dr. Hiluluk... medicine really can cure the world."
  3. **Robin's Broadcast** — Robin broadcasts the complete truth of the Void Century. In Ohara's ruins, a burned tree suddenly blooms.
  4. **Nami's Map** — The world's geography is fundamentally different. Her dream — mapping the entire world — is now possible because the world is whole.
  5. **Franky's Legacy** — Franky pats the Sunny: "You did it, partner."
  6. **Usopp Calls Kaya** — "This time... everything I tell you will be true." She's crying. He's crying.
  7. **Brook's Journey Home** — Brook looks toward the Twin Capes. "I'm coming, old friend."
  8. **The Party** — The biggest party the world has ever seen. Pirates, Marines, revolutionaries, kings, fishmen, giants — everyone celebrates together.
  9. **Quiet Conversations** — Harry and Shanks share another drink. Dragon watches from a distance. Sabo tells stories about Ace.
  10. **Bonney and Kuma** — Using Kuma's preserved memory bubbles and Harry's Nika resonance, they restore Kuma's consciousness. He holds Bonney: "...Bonney?" She throws herself into his arms. He's back.
  11. **The Straw Hat Returns** — Harry offers the hat to Shanks. Shanks puts it back on Harry's head. "Keep it. It was always yours."
  12. **Sunset** — Harry sits on the Sunny's figurehead watching the sky turn gold. His crew around him, alive, together, their dreams within reach.
- **Emotional Beat:** Kuma waking up and holding Bonney. The father who gave up everything — getting it all back.
- **Anime Moments:** Kuma's eyes focusing and seeing Bonney, Harry offering the straw hat to Shanks, the crew at sunset
