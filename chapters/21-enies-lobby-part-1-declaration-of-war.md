# Chapter 21: Enies Lobby (Part 1) - Declaration of War

- **Setting:** Enies Lobby — the World Government's judicial island, the island that never sees darkness
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew (minus Usopp, plus Franky and Galley-La allies), Robin, Spandam, CP9
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Fortress** — Enies Lobby is terrifying — a judicial island that never sees darkness, surrounded by a massive whirlpool, defended by 10,000 soldiers, the Gates of Justice, and CP9. The crew crashes through the front gate on the Rocketman. Ten thousand Marines mobilize. Let Harry see the scale of what they are attacking — and charge in anyway.
  2. **The Assault** — The crew storms the island. Harry punches through platoons. Zoro cuts through squads. Sanji kicks through barricades. Usopp — who is NOT officially part of the crew anymore — appears wearing a mask and cape, calling himself "Sogeking, the King of Snipers from Sniper Island." Everyone knows it is Usopp. Nobody says anything.
  3. **Robin's Backstory — Ohara (Part 1)** — The story of Robin's past unfolds. Ohara was an island of scholars — the greatest library in the world. The scholars studied the Poneglyphs and the Void Century. The World Government declared this knowledge forbidden. Robin was eight years old, an outcast child with the Flower-Flower Fruit, mocked by other kids for being a "devil child."
  4. **Robin's Backstory — Ohara (Part 2)** — Young Robin's only friends were the scholars, especially Professor Clover and a giant named Jaguar D. Saul. Saul taught Robin to laugh: "Dereshishishi!" The World Government ordered a Buster Call — complete annihilation. Ten warships. The library burned. The scholars died. Saul was frozen by Aokiji. Robin ran to the evacuation boat. Aokiji let her go — one survivor, as Saul's dying wish.
  5. **Robin's Backstory — 20 Years Alone (Part 3)** — Robin spent 20 years running. Every person who ever helped her was hunted down and killed by the Government. She learned to trust no one. She joined Baroque Works because she had nowhere else to go. And now she believes the Straw Hats will die because of her, just like everyone else. She surrendered to CP9 to save them.
  6. **The Bridge** — Robin stands on the far side of a judicial gap, handcuffed, surrounded by CP9 and Spandam. She tells the crew: "I cannot come with you. Do not save me. Eventually you will betray me too." She has given up.
  7. **Burn the Flag** — Harry's response. He does not argue. He does not beg. He turns to Sogeking (Usopp) and gives one order: "Shoot down that flag." Usopp loads a flaming ammunition into his slingshot, aims at the World Government flag flying above Enies Lobby, and shoots. The flag catches fire. It burns. This is a declaration of war against the World Government. The entire world will know what the Straw Hats did here.
  8. **"I WANT TO LIVE!"** — Harry shouts across the gap: "ROBIN! Say you want to live! Say it!" Robin breaks. Twenty years of walls crumble. The tears come. She screams with everything she has: "I WANT TO LIVE! TAKE ME TO THE SEA WITH YOU!" The crew charges. This is the single most iconic moment in One Piece.
- **Emotional Beat:** "I WANT TO LIVE!" — Robin's 20-year wall of isolation shattering in one scream. The crew declaring war on the entire World Government for one friend. Burning the flag.
- **Anime Moments:** The World Government flag burning, Robin screaming "I want to live!" with tears streaming, the crew charging across the gap
