# Chapter 23: Enies Lobby (Part 3) - Harry vs Rob Lucci

- **Setting:** Enies Lobby, the Tower of Justice
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Rob Lucci
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Harry Faces Lucci** — Lucci stands between Harry and Robin. Leopard hybrid form. Rokushiki mastered. Finger Pistol pierces Harry's rubber body. Iron Body makes Lucci nearly invulnerable. Harry is getting destroyed.
  2. **Gear 2** — Harry pumps his blood faster through his rubber vessels. Steam rises from his skin. "Gear Second." Jet Pistol — ten times normal speed. Harry lands hits for the first time. Jet Bazooka, Jet Gatling. Massive power but burns through stamina fast.
  3. **Gear 3** — Harry inflates his bones with air — his fist swells to giant size. "Gear Third." Gigant Pistol — a fist the size of a building. But afterward Harry shrinks to chibi-size temporarily.
  4. **Lucci's Assault** — Rokuougan — a shockwave that hits from inside, bypassing rubber. Harry vomits blood. His organs are damaged. He falls. He cannot move. Lucci walks toward Robin.
  5. **Usopp's Voice** — From across the island, through a Den Den Mushi: "Harry! GET UP! You are the man who will be KING OF THE PIRATES! There is no way you can lose here!" Harry hears his friend. His fingers twitch. He stands.
  6. **Jet Gatling** — Gear 2. Gum Gum Jet Gatling — hundreds of high-speed punches. Lucci cannot dodge them all. One final punch. Lucci falls. He does not get up.
  7. **The Buster Call** — Ten warships bombard Enies Lobby. Fire everywhere. No way out.
  8. **The Going Merry** — From the flames — a ship. No one at the helm. The Going Merry sailed herself to save her crew one last time. The Klabautermann — the spirit of the ship — brought her. The crew leaps aboard. They escape through the bombardment. One last voyage.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry getting up because of Usopp's voice. Jet Gatling. The Going Merry sailing herself to save the crew.
- **Anime Moments:** Steam rising as Gear 2 activates, the Jet Gatling barrage, the Going Merry appearing through the flames
