# Chapter 42: Dressrosa (Part 3) - Gear 4

- **Setting:** Dressrosa — the palace rooftop
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Doflamingo
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Harry Reaches the Palace** — Harry arrives at the palace rooftop where Doflamingo stands over Law's broken body. The Birdcage continues to shrink.
  2. **Doflamingo's Power** — Doflamingo fights at full strength. Harry fights back with Gear 2 and Gear 3 but cannot land a clean hit.
  3. **Awakening** — Doflamingo reveals Devil Fruit Awakening. Everything turns into strings. Harry is overwhelmed.
  4. **GEAR 4: BOUNDMAN** — Harry inflates with Armament Haki. His body becomes enormous — coated in swirling black Haki patterns. "GUM GUM — KONG GUN." The punch sends Doflamingo flying through the entire city. Doflamingo's sunglasses crack.
  5. **Boundman Dominates** — Gear 4 Harry is a force of nature. He flies by bouncing off the air. Rhino Schneider. Culverin. Doflamingo cannot keep up.
  6. **The Time Limit** — Gear 4 runs out. Harry's Haki drains completely. He is helpless for ten minutes.
  7. **The Country Protects Harry** — The people of Dressrosa — gladiators, dwarves, citizens — throw themselves between Doflamingo and Harry. Everyone Harry has helped stands up for him. Ten minutes. They just need ten minutes.
  8. **Law's Role** — Law fires a Gamma Knife into Doflamingo's internal organs. The damage compounds.
  9. **King Kong Gun** — Gear 4 returns. "GUM GUM — KING KONG GUN." The fist shatters through Doflamingo's strings and drives him into the bedrock. The Birdcage falls apart. Dressrosa is free.
  10. **The Aftermath** — The country erupts in celebration. The Marines arrive to arrest Doflamingo.
  11. **The Straw Hat Grand Fleet** — Seven pirate captains pledge their crews — over 5,000 warriors strong. Harry: "I do not want to be some big shot!" They pledge anyway.
  12. **New Bounty** — Harry's new bounty: 500 million berries.
- **Emotional Beat:** The entire country protecting Harry during his ten-minute weakness. The Grand Fleet forming against Harry's wishes.
- **Anime Moments:** Gear 4 Boundman revealed, Kong Gun sending Doflamingo crashing, King Kong Gun descending like a meteor
- **Crew After Chapter:** Full crew. Grand Fleet allied. Bounty: 500 million.
