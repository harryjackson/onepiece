# Chapter 64: The Race to Laugh Tale

- **Setting:** The waters approaching Laugh Tale, the shores of the final island
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Blackbeard (Marshall D. Teach) and his crew, Shanks (Red-Haired Pirates)
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **Landfall** — The crew steps onto Laugh Tale. It's lush, green, strangely peaceful.
  2. **Exploring the Island** — Overgrown ruins, strange formations. Robin finds stone tablets everywhere.
  3. **Roger's Traces** — Evidence of Roger's visit 25 years ago: his Jolly Roger carved into a tree.
  4. **The Central Chamber** — The complete, uncensored history of the Void Century. Robin begins reading and can't stop.
  5. **Blackbeard Arrives** — Marshall D. Teach and his crew storm the beaches. "Thanks for doing the hard work!"
  6. **Harry Confronts Teach** — The man who killed Ace. "You don't get to take anything else from anyone."
  7. **Crew vs. Blackbeard Pirates** — Zoro vs. Shiryu. Sanji vs. Avalo Pizarro. Jinbe vs. San Juan Wolf.
  8. **Shanks Appears** — "Go, Harry! Go see what Roger saw! That's an order from the man who gave you that hat!"
  9. **Harry vs. Blackbeard — Opening Clash** — Darkness vs. Nika. The fight reshapes Laugh Tale's landscape.
  10. **The Crew Holds the Line** — The crew battles to protect the Poneglyph chamber.
  11. **Robin's Translation** — Robin races to read everything before Blackbeard's crew can reach the room.
  12. **The Precipice** — Harry and Teach, battered, circling each other. "That's why you'll never be King."
- **Emotional Beat:** Shanks appearing to support Harry at the final island — the man who started Harry's journey arriving at the end.
- **Anime Moments:** Roger's Jolly Roger on a tree, Shanks arriving, Harry and Blackbeard clashing
