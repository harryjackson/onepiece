# Chapter 22: Enies Lobby (Part 2) - The Battles

- **Setting:** Enies Lobby, the Tower of Justice
- **Key Characters:** All crew vs CP9 members
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Tower of Justice** — The crew reaches the Tower of Justice. CP9 waits — each agent in a separate room. The crew must fight through them to reach Robin. Franky is freed and joins the fight. Each crew member faces their match.
  2. **Nami vs Kalifa** — Kalifa has the Bubble-Bubble Fruit. Nami is stripped of her ability to fight. But Nami uses her Clima-Tact to summon thunder and blasts Kalifa with a Thunderbolt Tempo. Nami wins through intelligence and weather science.
  3. **Chopper vs Kumadori** — Chopper uses his Rumble Ball transformations but cannot keep up. In desperation, Chopper takes a third Rumble Ball — triggering Monster Point, an uncontrollable giant form of immense power. He wins but loses all control.
  4. **Franky vs Fukurou** — A brawl between two powerhouses. Franky uses his cyborg weapons — Coup de Vent, Strong Right, cola-powered attacks. He runs out of cola mid-fight and must refuel. A fun, loud fight.
  5. **Sanji vs Jabra** — Jabra has the Wolf-Wolf Fruit. Sanji fights him with kicks alone. Mid-fight, Sanji unlocks Diable Jambe — by spinning at extreme speed, his leg catches fire. He defeats Jabra with a flaming kick combo. A major power-up for Sanji.
  6. **Sogeking (Usopp) in Battle** — Usopp, still masked as Sogeking, fights alongside other crew members. He provides ranged support, snipes targets from impossible distances, and sets up tactical advantages. He is terrified the entire time but never runs.
  7. **Zoro vs Kaku** — Kaku ate the Giraffe-Giraffe Fruit. Zoro is pushed to his limits. He unleashes Asura — a terrifying illusion technique where Zoro appears to have six arms, three heads, and nine swords. One devastating strike — Asura: Ichibugin. Kaku falls. Zoro's most powerful technique yet.
  8. **Spandam Drags Robin** — While the fights rage, Spandam drags Robin toward the Gate of Justice. He taunts her, hits her, and demonstrates everything wrong with the World Government. Robin endures in silence, waiting for her crew.
- **Emotional Beat:** Each crew member fighting beyond their limits for Robin. Sanji's Diable Jambe. Zoro's Asura. The crew earning their way to their friend.
- **Anime Moments:** Sanji's leg catching fire for Diable Jambe, Zoro's Asura nine-sword illusion, Chopper's terrifying Monster Point
