# Chapter 29: Impel Down (Part 1) - The Underwater Prison

- **Setting:** Impel Down — the world's most secure prison, deep under the ocean. Six levels, each worse than the last.
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Bon Clay (Mr. 2), Buggy (returns), Mr. 3 (returns), Magellan (Warden, Poison-Poison Fruit), Ivankov (Revolutionary Army)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Infiltration** — Hancock smuggles Harry into Impel Down hidden under her cloak. She bluffs past every checkpoint.
  2. **Level 1: Crimson Hell** — A forest of razor-sharp trees. Harry pushes through, rubber body deflecting the blades.
  3. **Buggy Returns** — Harry runs into Buggy the Clown and Mr. 3, both locked up. An unlikely team forms.
  4. **Level 2: Beast Hell** — Massive creatures roam the corridors. Harry fights through.
  5. **Bon Clay: The Truest Friend** — Harry reunites with Bon Clay. He dances his Swan dance. Harry laughs for the first time since losing his crew.
  6. **Levels 3 and 4** — Starvation Hell and Blazing Hell — each more terrible than the last.
  7. **Magellan: The Poison Warden** — Magellan's Hydra technique coats Harry in deadly venom. Harry collapses, dying. Bon Clay carries his limp body deeper.
  8. **Level 5: Freezing Hell** — Bon Clay drags Harry through the frozen wasteland. Harry is unconscious. Bon Clay's tears freeze on his face. He refuses to let go.
  9. **Ivankov Saves Harry** — Ivankov's Hormone-Hormone Fruit can cure Harry but warns: 10 years off his lifespan, 20 hours of agony, 2-3% survival. Bon Clay: "DO IT."
  10. **Twenty Hours of Screaming** — Harry screams for twenty hours during the treatment. Bon Clay stands outside screaming encouragement. Then the door smashes open. Harry: "FOOD! I NEED MEAT!" He survived.
- **Emotional Beat:** Bon Clay carrying Harry through Freezing Hell. The twenty hours of screaming. Harry's refusal to die.
- **Anime Moments:** Magellan's Hydra engulfing Harry, Bon Clay carrying Harry through ice, Harry bursting out demanding meat
