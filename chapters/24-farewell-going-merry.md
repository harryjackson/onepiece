# Chapter 24: Farewell, Going Merry

- **Setting:** The sea outside Enies Lobby
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, the Going Merry
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Merry Cannot Go On** — The crew escapes the Buster Call on the Going Merry. But the ship is breaking apart in real time. The keel snaps. The mast cracks. Wood splinters under their feet. She carried them one final time, and now she is dying.
  2. **The Viking Funeral** — They transfer to a Marine ship they commandeered. Harry stands before the Going Merry one last time. He lights the fire himself. The Merry burns on the open sea. The flames climb the mast, catch the sail, consume the figurehead.
  3. **The Merry Speaks** — As the Merry burns, a voice emerges — the spirit of the ship, the Klabautermann. A soft, sad, grateful voice: "I am sorry. I wanted to carry you all just a little further." The entire crew breaks down. Chopper sobs. Nami covers her face. Zoro turns away. Sanji hides his tears. Usopp screams. Harry, tears pouring, screams back: "I'M SORRY! WE BROKE YOU! YOU WERE THE BEST SHIP EVER!" The Merry sinks into the sea, burning. Let Harry feel this loss — this is the death of a friend, not a thing.
  4. **Recovery in Water 7** — The crew recovers at Water 7. Wounds heal. Spirits slowly lift. Iceburg is alive and recovering. The city now respects the crew for exposing CP9.
  5. **Franky Builds the Thousand Sunny** — Franky uses the 200 million berries to buy Adam Wood — the rarest, strongest shipbuilding material. He builds the Thousand Sunny. She is magnificent — a lion-headed figurehead, Coup de Burst propulsion, a lawn deck, an aquarium, a kitchen Sanji adores, a sick bay for Chopper, a library for Robin, a workshop for Franky and Usopp. She is their new home.
  6. **Usopp's Apology** — Usopp wants to rejoin the crew. Harry is firm: Usopp left. He challenged the captain and lost. If he wants back, he has to apologize. Zoro supports this. As the Sunny prepares to sail, Usopp stands on the shore, tears streaming, and screams: "I AM SORRY! I WAS WRONG! PLEASE LET ME SAIL WITH YOU AGAIN!" Harry, already crying, stretches his arm and pulls Usopp aboard: "WHAT TOOK YOU SO LONG, IDIOT!" The crew is whole again.
  7. **Franky Joins** — Franky wants to go but claims he cannot leave Water 7. The Franky Family steal his swimming trunks and hold them hostage on the Thousand Sunny. Franky, naked and chasing them, ends up on the ship as it sails away. He gives in: "FINE! I will build you the greatest ship in the world!" Franky officially joins as the shipwright.
  8. **New Bounties** — Harry: 300 million berries. Zoro: 120 million. The world now knows the Straw Hats declared war on the World Government.
- **Emotional Beat:** The Going Merry's death. "I wanted to carry you just a little further." Usopp's apology. The crew whole again. The Thousand Sunny as a new beginning.
- **Anime Moments:** The Going Merry burning at sea, the Merry's spirit speaking, Usopp screaming his apology from the shore
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky
- **Ship:** Thousand Sunny
