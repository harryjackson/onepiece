# Chapter 13: Alabasta (Part 2) - Crocodile Falls

- **Setting:** Alabasta — Alubarna (capital city) and the Royal Tomb
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Crocodile, Robin, Vivi
- **Key Events:**
  - Harry fights Crocodile — and LOSES. Crocodile is a Logia (sand). Harry can't touch him. Crocodile impales Harry with his hook and leaves him for dead
  - Harry survives and fights Crocodile again — discovers water makes sand solid. Fights with water-soaked fists. Loses again. Crocodile poisons him.
  - Robin saves Harry (why?)
  - Third fight — Harry uses his own blood to harden the sand. Final blow: Gum Gum Storm — a barrage of punches that sends Crocodile through solid bedrock
  - Vivi stops the war. Rain falls on Alabasta for the first time in years.
  - Vivi must choose: sail with the crew, or stay and rebuild her kingdom
  - The farewell: Vivi stands on the wall, crying, asking "Will you still call me a friend?!" The crew stands with their backs turned, raising their arms with the X marks they drew — a silent promise. No words needed.
  - Robin appears on the ship uninvited and asks to join. Harry says yes immediately.
  - Harry's new bounty: 100 million berries
- **Scenes:**
  1. **First Fight — Sand Cannot Be Touched** — Harry confronts Crocodile. Sand-Sand Fruit, a Logia. His body IS sand. Harry's Gum Gum Pistol passes through Crocodile's chest. Nothing works. Crocodile drains Harry's moisture, impales him on his golden hook, and throws him into quicksand. Harry sinks. Let Harry feel his first real defeat against a Logia — total helplessness.
  2. **Saved by Water** — Harry survives. He realizes water solidifies sand into mud. He fills his body with water and goes after Crocodile again.
  3. **Second Fight — Water Fists** — Harry soaks his fists and can land hits. But Crocodile is still vastly stronger. Ground Secco drains moisture from everything. He poisons Harry with his hook. Harry collapses. Robin finds him dying and gives him the antidote. Why? She will not say.
  4. **The Underground Tomb** — Crocodile wants Pluton — an ancient weapon hidden in Alabasta. He forces King Cobra to show the Poneglyph. Robin reads it and lies about the contents. Crocodile stabs her. Even his own partner is disposable.
  5. **Third Fight — Blood and Fury** — Harry arrives beaten twice, poisoned, running on willpower. No water left. But blood works too. Harry coats his fists in his own blood and attacks. Crocodile: "Why won't you DIE?!" Harry: "Because I made a promise to Vivi."
  6. **Gum Gum Storm** — Harry launches upward. A barrage of blood-soaked punches sends Crocodile through the tomb ceiling, through bedrock, into the sky. Water erupts from underground. Rain falls on Alabasta for the first time in years. Soldiers stop fighting. They look up. They cry.
  7. **Vivi Stops the War** — Vivi screams: "PLEASE STOP FIGHTING!" With rain falling, the soldiers hear her. The war is over. Let Harry, battered in the rubble, hear it and smile.
  8. **The Crew Recovers** — The crew gathers in the palace, all wounded, all victorious. Enormous feast. Chopper treats wounds. Sanji cooks. Harry eats 47 plates. Usopp tells exaggerated stories. A peaceful moment before the goodbye.
  9. **Vivi's Choice** — Vivi must choose: sail with the crew or stay and rebuild her kingdom. She loves them. She wants to go. But millions need their princess. She stays.
  10. **The Farewell — X Marks** — The crew sails away. Vivi runs to the palace wall, crying: "WILL YOU STILL CALL ME YOUR FRIEND?!" The crew stands with backs turned. Silently, they raise their left arms — the X marks. No words needed. Vivi raises her own X mark. Arms stay raised until neither side can see the other. One of the most iconic moments in all of One Piece.
  11. **Robin Joins** — Nico Robin is sitting on the ship, reading. "I have nowhere to go. Take responsibility." Harry: "Okay. You can join." Nami screams. Zoro does not trust her. But Harry decided.
  12. **New Bounties** — Harry: 100 million berries. Sanji's photo is a terrible sketch. Chopper gets 50 berries (they think he is a pet). The adventure in Alabasta is over.
- **Emotional Beat:** The farewell to Vivi — the X marks on their arms. Harry defeating a Warlord through sheer refusal to die. Rain falling on Alabasta.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry punching Crocodile with blood-soaked fists, Gum Gum Storm through bedrock, the crew raising X-marked arms as Vivi cries
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin
