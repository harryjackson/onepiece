# Chapter 59: Egghead Island (Part 3) - Vegapunk's Message

- **Setting:** Egghead Island — the Labophase under siege
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Vegapunk, Kizaru, Saturn, York (traitor), Stussy, Sentomaru, CP0
- **Scenes:**
  1. **York's Betrayal Revealed** — York made a deal with the Gorosei. She wants to become a Celestial Dragon. Shaka is shot.
  2. **The Crew Splits** — Multiple crises across the island. Zoro fights awakened Lucci. Sanji and Franky free trapped crew members.
  3. **Zoro vs. Lucci** — Zoro defeats awakened Lucci with advanced Conqueror's Haki coating.
  4. **Stussy's Secret** — Stussy is a clone of a legendary Rocks pirate, secretly protecting Vegapunk.
  5. **Kizaru Enters the Labophase** — Kizaru defeats Sentomaru with tears on his face. Duty over friendship.
  6. **Harry vs. Kizaru — Round One** — Harry activates Gear 5. They trade blows that shatter the Labophase walls. Kizaru is genuinely shocked.
  7. **Vegapunk's Fatal Wound** — Saturn stabs Vegapunk. But Vegapunk activated a dead man's switch — a pre-recorded message will broadcast to the entire world.
  8. **The Broadcast Begins** — Vegapunk's face appears on screens worldwide. "My name is Dr. Vegapunk. I am about to die. Before I go, there is something the world must know."
  9. **The Void Century Truths** — Vegapunk reveals: the Ancient Kingdom, the Twenty Kings, Joyboy's promise. The world stops and listens.
  10. **The Other Gorosei Arrive** — All five members of the Gorosei appear in their true demonic forms. Five ancient demons, each radiating overwhelming power.
  11. **Overwhelming Power** — The Gorosei regenerate from every wound. The crew cannot win this battle. Vegapunk: "Run, Harry. Live, and find the One Piece."
  12. **Harry's Choice** — Harry orders the crew to fall back to the Sunny. "WE'RE LEAVING! EVERYONE TO THE SHIP! NOW!"
- **Emotional Beat:** Vegapunk's sacrifice — choosing to die so the world can hear the truth. Harry choosing to retreat for his crew's sake.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry in Gear 5 clashing with Kizaru, Vegapunk's face on screens across the world, all five Gorosei in demon forms
