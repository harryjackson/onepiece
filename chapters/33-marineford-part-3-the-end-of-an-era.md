# Chapter 33: Marineford (Part 3) - The End of an Era

- **Setting:** Marineford
- **Key Characters:** Harry (unconscious), Whitebeard, Blackbeard, Shanks, Koby, Law
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Whitebeard Fights On** — Whitebeard stands his ground alone against the full force of the Marines. He takes wounds that would kill any normal person ten times over. He does not fall. He does not take a single step backward.
  2. **Akainu Pursues Harry** — Akainu throws magma at Jinbe. Jinbe falls, shielding Harry. Crocodile intervenes. Buggy grabs Harry and Jinbe and flees.
  3. **Law's Submarine** — Trafalgar Law surfaces in his submarine. "I am a doctor. I will save Straw Hat." Harry and Jinbe are brought aboard. Law dives.
  4. **Blackbeard Arrives** — Marshall D. Teach arrives with criminals from Level 6. He stands before Whitebeard. Whitebeard is furious: "You are the one thing I cannot forgive."
  5. **Whitebeard's Last Stand** — Whitebeard attacks Blackbeard with everything he has left. The Blackbeard Pirates swarm Whitebeard. He takes every hit, refusing to fall.
  6. **"THE ONE PIECE IS REAL!"** — Whitebeard faces the cameras. His final words echo across every sea: **"THE ONE PIECE IS REAL!"** He dies standing upright. 267 sword wounds, 152 bullets, 46 cannonballs. Not a single wound on his back. He never ran.
  7. **Blackbeard Steals the Power** — Blackbeard somehow steals the Tremor-Tremor Fruit. He now has two Devil Fruit powers — the only person in history to do so.
  8. **Koby's Courage** — Young Marine Koby steps in front of Akainu. "Stop it! Every soldier who falls now has a family waiting!" Akainu raises his magma fist.
  9. **Shanks Arrives** — A sword blocks Akainu's fist. Red-Haired Shanks. "I have come to end this war." No one challenges him. The Admirals back down. The war is over.
  10. **The Aftermath** — Law's submarine carries Harry away. The era of the old legends is finished. A new, darker era begins.
- **Emotional Beat:** Whitebeard dying standing — not a single wound on his back. "THE ONE PIECE IS REAL." Koby's courage. Shanks ending the war.
- **Anime Moments:** Whitebeard dying on his feet, Shanks blocking Akainu's attack, Koby screaming at Akainu
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry unconscious on Law's submarine. Crew scattered. Ace dead. Whitebeard dead.
