# Chapter 44: Whole Cake Island (Part 1) - Big Mom's Territory

- **Setting:** Totto Land — Big Mom's territory, a land made of sweets and food
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Nami, Chopper, Brook, Pedro, Carrot, Sanji, Big Mom, Katakuri, Pudding, the Vinsmoke Family
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Entering Totto Land** — Everything is made of sweets. Big Mom's Soul-Soul Fruit has given life to objects — a cheerful, terrifying wonderland.
  2. **The Seducing Woods** — A forest where trees talk and paths rearrange. Nami uses Big Mom's vivre card to intimidate the forest homies.
  3. **Charlotte Cracker** — Harry fights him for eleven hours straight. He wins by eating the biscuit soldiers until he can use a tank-like Gear 4 form.
  4. **Sanji's Backstory: The Vinsmoke Family** — Young Sanji was the "failure" — he could feel. His father locked him in a dungeon with an iron mask. His mother died protecting him. His sister Reiju freed him. He escaped to find Zeff.
  5. **Sanji Pushes Harry Away** — Sanji kicks Harry. Insults him. Tells him to leave. Each blow is designed to make Harry hate him. Harry does not fight back. He takes every kick.
  6. **"I Cannot Become King of the Pirates Without You"** — Harry: "I know you did not mean any of that." He sits down in the rain. "I will wait right here. And I will not eat anything until you come back to cook for me."
  7. **Sanji Breaks** — Sanji discovers the wedding is a trap. Big Mom plans to assassinate the entire Vinsmoke family. He makes his choice: save everyone.
  8. **Brook's Mission** — Brook infiltrates Big Mom's treasure room alone, fights Big Mom herself, and makes copies of the Poneglyphs. "I got them." The biggest underdog achievement in the entire series.
  9. **Pudding's Betrayal and Redemption** — Sanji calls her Third Eye beautiful. She breaks down and cannot go through with the betrayal.
  10. **Pedro's Sacrifice** — Pedro charges Perospero and ignites explosives strapped to his body. He dies to buy the crew time to escape.
- **Emotional Beat:** "I cannot become King of the Pirates without you" — Harry sitting in the rain, starving, waiting for Sanji. Sanji's backstory. Pedro's sacrifice. Brook standing alone against an Emperor.
- **Anime Moments:** Harry sitting in the rain, Brook standing over the Poneglyph copies, Pedro's explosion
