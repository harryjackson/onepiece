# Chapter 1: Romance Dawn - The Boy and the Straw Hat

- **Setting:** Foosha Village, East Blue
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Red-Haired Shanks, Shanks's crew (Beckman, Lucky Roux, Yasopp), Higuma (mountain bandit), Makino (bartender), Mayor Woop Slap
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Pirates Are in Town** — Harry bursts into Makino's bar where Shanks and his crew are drinking, laughing, and telling stories. The bar is warm and loud. Harry demands Shanks take him on the next voyage. Shanks laughs and says he's too young. Harry pouts. Let Harry argue his case — why should Shanks take him? Shanks's crew teases Harry. Yasopp brags about his son back home (Usopp). Lucky Roux is eating meat. Beckman smokes quietly and watches.
  2. **The Gum Gum Fruit** — Harry finds a strange purple fruit in a treasure chest on the bar counter. It looks weird and swirly. He's hungry and annoyed at Shanks, so he eats the whole thing. Shanks turns around and PANICS — "YOU ATE THAT?!" Harry's body starts stretching like rubber. His cheeks pull out a foot. His arms wobble. He's a rubber boy now — and he can never swim again. Let Harry react to his new rubber body — try stretching, bouncing, pulling his face. It's weird and funny and a little scary.
  3. **Shanks and the Mountain Bandits** — Higuma and his gang swagger into the bar. They insult Shanks, pour sake on his head, smash bottles. Shanks just laughs, wipes his face, and offers to clean up. Harry is FURIOUS — why won't Shanks fight back?! After the bandits leave, Shanks explains: "There are some fights not worth having. A real man knows when to let things go." Harry doesn't understand this yet. Let Harry argue about it — this lesson will come back later.
  4. **Harry Stands Up** — Days later. The bandits return to the village. Higuma insults Shanks again in front of Harry. This time Harry steps forward and yells at Higuma, defending Shanks's honor. Higuma is furious — a KID talking back? He grabs Harry. The villagers are terrified. Makino runs for help. Let Harry say something brave and reckless to Higuma.
  5. **Thrown Into the Sea** — Higuma drags Harry to the harbor and throws him into the ocean. Harry can't swim — the Devil Fruit has taken that from him forever. He sinks. He panics. The water presses in from all sides. He's drowning. Then a massive shadow rises from beneath — a Sea King, a monster of the deep. Its jaws open. This is real danger. Harry is helpless.
  6. **Shanks Saves Harry** — Shanks appears in the water. He grabs Harry with one arm and stares down the Sea King. The monster freezes — Shanks's eyes are terrifying (this is Conqueror's Haki, though Harry doesn't know it yet). The Sea King retreats. But when Harry looks up, Shanks is bleeding. His left arm is gone — bitten off by the Sea King. Shanks is smiling. "It's just an arm. I bet it on the new era." Harry breaks down crying. Let Harry react — guilt, fear, admiration, love.
  7. **The Straw Hat** — Shanks is leaving. His ship is packed. Harry stands on the dock, crying, promising he'll become a great pirate. Shanks takes off his straw hat and puts it on Harry's head. "Give it back to me someday... when you've become a great pirate." The hat is too big. It covers Harry's eyes. He's sobbing. Shanks's ship sails away. Harry watches until it disappears. He clutches the hat. This is the most important object in the world to him now.
  8. **Ten Years Later — Setting Sail** — Time skip. Harry is 17. He stands in a tiny boat in Foosha Village harbor. Makino waves goodbye. Mayor Woop Slap yells that he's an idiot. Harry grins, wearing the straw hat, and pushes off into the East Blue. He has no crew, no map, no plan. Just a dream. Let Harry declare his dream out loud to the empty ocean: "I'M GOING TO BE KING OF THE PIRATES!" A whirlpool appears ahead. Harry has no idea how to navigate. The adventure begins.
- **Emotional Beat:** Shanks sacrificing his arm. The straw hat promise. Harry setting sail alone with nothing but a dream.
- **Anime Moments:** Shanks staring down the Sea King, the straw hat being placed on Harry's head, Harry setting sail alone
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry
- **Harry's Goal Established:** "I'm going to be King of the Pirates!"
