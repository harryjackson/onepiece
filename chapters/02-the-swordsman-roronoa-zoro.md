# Chapter 2: The Swordsman - Roronoa Zoro

- **Setting:** Shells Town, East Blue
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Zoro, Koby, Captain Morgan, Helmeppo, Rika, Alvida
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Adrift at Sea** — Harry is lost on the ocean in his tiny boat. No food, no compass. A whirlpool appears. He ends up in a barrel that washes onto Alvida's ship. He bursts out and meets Koby — a terrified kid mopping the deck. Koby has been Alvida's cabin boy (basically a slave) for two years. He's skinny, scared, and apologizes for everything. Harry eats all the food in the storeroom while Koby panics.
  2. **Koby's Dream** — Harry and Koby talk below deck. Koby reveals his dream of becoming a Marine but says it's impossible — Alvida would kill him. Harry stares at him: "Why is it impossible?" This is Harry's first time inspiring someone. Let Harry encourage Koby in his own words. Koby is shaking but something changes in his eyes. He whispers: "I... I want to be a Marine."
  3. **Alvida Attacks** — Alvida appears on deck. She's enormous, carrying an iron club that cracks the wood beneath her. She demands to know who ate her food. She asks Koby: "Who is the most beautiful woman on the seas?" Koby always says "You are, Lady Alvida." But this time, with Harry beside him, Koby screams: "YOU'RE THE UGLIEST HAG ON THE OCEAN!" Alvida swings her club. Harry steps in front, takes the hit — the club bounces off his rubber body. He grins and punches her off the ship. First real Gum Gum Pistol in combat. Let Harry pick his own battle cry.
  4. **Sailing to Shells Town** — Harry and Koby escape in a small boat. Koby explains who Roronoa Zoro is — "Pirate Hunter Zoro, a demon who cuts down pirates for sport. They say he's a monster in human form." Harry immediately wants him on the crew. Koby is terrified: "Are you INSANE?!" They discuss what the Marines are, what Shells Town is like, and whether Zoro is really a demon. Harry is excited. Koby is sweating.
  5. **The Town of Fear** — Arriving at Shells Town. The streets are quiet. People flinch when anyone says "Morgan." A little girl named Rika mentions "the man tied up at the base" and the townspeople shush her frantically. Something is very wrong here — the Marines are supposed to protect people, but everyone is afraid of them. Harry and Koby explore the town. Let Harry talk to the scared townspeople and piece together what's happening.
  6. **The Man Tied to the Post** — Harry climbs the Marine base wall and sees Zoro for the first time. Tied to a wooden post in the yard, baking in the sun. No food, no water. He's been there for weeks. But his eyes are fierce — not broken, not begging. Zoro tells Harry to get lost. Harry tries to talk to him. Zoro explains: Helmeppo's wolf was running loose in town and almost killed Rika. Zoro killed the wolf. Helmeppo (Captain Morgan's spoiled son) made a deal — survive tied to the post for one month with no food, and Rika goes free. Zoro is nine days in. Let Harry try to convince Zoro to join his crew. Zoro refuses.
  7. **Rika's Rice Balls** — Little Rika climbs over the wall with rice balls she made herself — they're lumpy and sweet (she put sugar in them instead of salt, because she's a kid). She offers them to Zoro with a big smile. Helmeppo appears, slaps the rice balls out of her hands, stomps them into the dirt. He laughs and reveals he was never going to honor the deal — Zoro's execution is scheduled for tomorrow. A Marine throws Rika over the wall. After Helmeppo leaves, Zoro calls out to the Marine: "The rice balls on the ground... give them to me." He eats the dirty, stomped rice balls. "Tell the girl... it was delicious."
  8. **The Decision** — Harry must decide: break into a Marine base to save a pirate hunter he just met. Koby tries to stop him — "You can't attack the Marines! They're the law!" Harry goes anyway. He doesn't care about law or pirate or Marine. Zoro doesn't deserve to die. Let Harry make his choice and explain why.
  9. **Stealing the Swords** — Harry rockets into the Marine base using Gum Gum Rocket. He crashes through a window, bounces off walls, tumbles through hallways. He finds Helmeppo's fancy bedroom and spots three katanas on a rack. He grabs all three — he doesn't know which one is Zoro's, so he takes them all. Helmeppo walks in and screams for guards. The alarm sounds. Marines pour out of every door.
  10. **The Firing Squad** — In the yard, Morgan orders Zoro's immediate execution. Marines line up with rifles aimed at Zoro. Zoro faces them without flinching — he's ready to die. Harry leaps from the roof, lands between Zoro and the rifles. The Marines fire. Bullets bounce off Harry's rubber body and ricochet everywhere. Marines dive for cover. Harry grins: "That didn't work." He holds out the three swords: "I don't know which one's yours." Zoro: "All three. I use three-sword style."
  11. **Zoro Unleashed** — Harry cuts Zoro's ropes. Zoro takes his swords — one in each hand, one in his mouth. Three-sword style. Twenty Marines charge. One slash. All twenty go down. Harry fights Captain Morgan — the axe-hand Marine captain. Morgan is strong but slow. Harry dodges, stretches, and finishes him with a Gum Gum Pistol that sends Morgan crashing through the base wall. The townspeople cheer. The Marines who hated Morgan cheer too.
  12. **Zoro's Pledge** — After the battle, in the town. Zoro sits across from Harry. He's eating his first real meal in weeks. He looks at Harry seriously. "I'll join your crew. But I have one condition. I'm going to become the world's greatest swordsman. If you ever do anything that gets in the way of that dream... I'll cut you down." Harry's response defines their relationship. Let Harry answer — does he accept the condition? What does he say? This is the founding bond of the crew.
  13. **Farewell to Koby** — Koby announces he's staying at the Marine base to enlist. He's crying but standing tall for the first time. Harry grins. Koby salutes — a sloppy, imperfect salute, but it's real. They promise to meet again someday, on opposite sides of the law. Harry and Zoro sail away. Two pirates. One dream each. The crew has begun.
- **Emotional Beat:** Zoro eating the dirty rice balls. Zoro's loyalty pledge. Koby finding courage and saluting goodbye.
- **Anime Moments:** Zoro three-sword style first reveal against the Marines, Harry tanking bullets with his rubber body, Koby saluting goodbye
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro
