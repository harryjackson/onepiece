# Chapter 16: Skypiea (Part 2) - Rubber vs Lightning

- **Setting:** Upper Yard, the Golden Bell
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Enel, Wyper, Robin, Gan Fall
- **Key Events:**
  - Enel's plan revealed: he wants to destroy all of Skypiea and sail to the moon (the "Fairy Vearth")
  - Enel systematically eliminates fighters — his Observation Haki (Mantra) lets him hear everything on the island
  - Robin finds the Poneglyph — ancient history connected to the Golden City of Shandora
  - Enel shocks everyone with massive lightning. Seems invincible.
  - Harry faces Enel. Enel hits him with lightning — nothing happens. Rubber doesn't conduct electricity. Harry is Enel's natural enemy.
  - Enel's face = the most iconic shocked face in anime history
  - Epic battle through the sky. Enel attaches a giant gold ball to Harry's arm. Harry uses it as a weapon.
  - Harry rings the Golden Bell with a massive punch through the clouds — the sound carries all the way down to Jaya. Cricket hears it. Noland was telling the truth. The city of gold was real — it was in the sky.
  - Skypiea is freed. The Shandians and sky people reconcile after 400 years
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Enel's Judgment** — Enel systematically eliminates fighters. His Mantra lets him hear every heartbeat on the island. He strikes from anywhere with lightning. Shandians fall. Sky people fall. His own priests fall. He predicted only five would survive and is engineering that outcome.
  2. **Enel's Plan** — He wants to destroy Skypiea and sail to the moon on a gold-powered ship. Millions will die. He does not care.
  3. **The Crew Falls** — One by one, crew members are struck down. Wyper uses the Reject Dial to stop Enel's heart. Enel restarts it with his own lightning. He is practically unkillable.
  4. **Harry vs Enel — Natural Enemy** — Enel hits Harry with 200 million volts. Harry stands there grinning. "That tickled." Enel's face — the most iconic shocked face in anime history. Rubber does not conduct electricity. Harry is the one person immune to Enel's power. Let Harry feel this moment.
  5. **The Battle in the Sky** — Enel fights with lightning, Mantra, and his golden staff. He melts gold onto Harry's right arm — a massive ball weighing hundreds of pounds. Harry is dragged down.
  6. **The Gold Weapon** — Harry turns the gold ball into a wrecking ball, swinging it with his rubber arm. The fight moves upward through the clouds toward the Golden Bell.
  7. **Ringing the Golden Bell** — Harry delivers a massive punch through the clouds, hitting the Golden Bell. BONG. The sound echoes all the way down to Jaya. Cricket hears it. He falls to his knees. His ancestor was right. The city of gold was in the sky. 400 years of shame — vindicated.
  8. **Skypiea Is Free** — Enel is defeated. The Shandians and sky people reconcile after 400 years of war. Feast on the clouds. The crew takes gold (Nami makes sure). Let Harry celebrate with the sky people.
  9. **The Descent** — The crew rides down through the clouds on a giant octopus balloon. They watch the sky island disappear. Gold, Dials, and memories of a world no one will believe they visited.
- **Emotional Beat:** Cricket hearing the bell from below the clouds. 400 years of being called liars — vindicated by a bell ringing from heaven. Harry being immune to a God.
- **Anime Moments:** Enel's iconic shocked face, Harry punching the Golden Bell with the gold-covered arm, Cricket hearing the bell from below
