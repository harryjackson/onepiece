# Chapter 32: Marineford (Part 2) - Fire and Magma

- **Setting:** Marineford — the execution platform
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Ace, Whitebeard, Akainu, Garp, Sengoku
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Final Push** — Harry is running out of strength. Whitebeard's crew covers him. Marco carries him part of the way. Jinbe guards his back.
  2. **Sengoku Orders the Execution** — The executioners raise their blades. Harry screams. Crocodile intercepts the blow.
  3. **Garp's Choice** — Harry reaches the stairs. Garp stands between him and Ace. Tears pour down his face. Harry's fist comes forward — and Garp lets it land. He chose family.
  4. **Harry Frees Ace** — Mr. 3 creates a wax key. Harry unlocks Ace's handcuffs. The Fire-Fire Fruit blazes to life. Ace is FREE.
  5. **Brothers Fighting Together** — Ace and Harry fight back to back. Fire and rubber combining. They are laughing, crying, together.
  6. **Akainu's Taunt** — Akainu insults Whitebeard. Ace stops. He cannot let anyone insult the man who called him "son." He turns to face Akainu.
  7. **Fire vs Magma** — Magma burns hotter than fire. Akainu's fist pushes through Ace's flames. Ace is overpowered.
  8. **Ace's Sacrifice** — Akainu throws a magma fist at Harry. Ace moves. He throws himself between Harry and the magma fist. The fist punches through Ace's chest.
  9. **"Thank You for Loving Me"** — Ace falls into Harry's arms. "I have just one regret... that I will not live to see you fulfill your dream." He looks at Harry. "Thank you for loving me." Ace is dead. The fire goes out.
  10. **Harry Breaks** — Harry screams. Not a battle cry — a raw, broken scream. His mind shatters. He cannot move. He cannot fight. His eyes go blank.
  11. **Jinbe Carries Harry** — Jinbe grabs Harry's limp body and runs. Akainu pursues. Jinbe takes the hit — burned across his chest — but keeps running.
- **Emotional Beat:** Ace's death. "Thank you for loving me." The single most devastating moment in One Piece. Harry's scream. His complete breakdown.
- **Anime Moments:** Akainu's magma fist punching through Ace's chest, Ace dying in Harry's arms, Harry's scream
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry broken, carried by Jinbe. Ace dead. The war continues.
