# Chapter 18: Water 7 (Part 1) - The City on the Water

- **Setting:** Water 7 — a beautiful city of shipwrights and canals
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Iceburg, Franky, Galley-La shipwrights, CP9 (hidden)
- **Key Events:**
  - Arrive at Water 7 to repair the Going Merry
  - Meet Iceburg — mayor and head of Galley-La Company, the world's best shipwrights
  - Devastating news: the Going Merry cannot be repaired. The keel is broken. The ship is dying.
  - Harry refuses to accept it. Usopp refuses even harder — the Merry was a gift from Kaya, it's everything to him
  - The crew's money (200 million berries from Skypiea gold) is stolen by the Franky Family
  - Usopp goes to get the money back alone and gets beaten badly
  - Harry decides to get a new ship. Usopp SNAPS. He challenges Harry to a duel for the Going Merry.
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The City of Water** — Water 7 is stunning — a Venice-like city built on water, with canals instead of streets, water elevators called Bull lifts, and beautiful architecture. The Galley-La Company runs everything. Let Harry explore the city and ride the canals, marvel at the water elevators, and eat local food.
  2. **Meeting Iceburg** — Iceburg is the mayor and head of Galley-La. He is calm, professional, and commands immense respect. The crew brings the Going Merry for repairs. Iceburg's shipwrights inspect the Merry and deliver the devastating news: the keel — the spine of the ship — is broken beyond repair. The Merry will not survive another voyage. She is dying.
  3. **The Crew Divides** — Harry is stunned but accepts the truth: they need a new ship. Usopp cannot accept it. The Merry was a gift from Kaya — it is his connection to Syrup Village, to his past, to the person who believed in him. Usopp: "We can't just throw her away!" Harry: "She can't sail anymore." The argument builds. The tension in the crew is palpable. Let Harry try to explain his decision.
  4. **The Franky Family** — While the crew argues, the 200 million berries is stolen by the Franky Family — a gang of ship dismantlers and bounty hunters led by a mysterious cyborg named Franky. Usopp was carrying the money. He chases the Franky Family alone to get it back. They beat him savagely and leave him bloody in the street.
  5. **The Crew Retaliates** — Harry, Zoro, Sanji, and Chopper storm the Franky Family's hideout and demolish it. They crush every member. But the money is gone — Franky already spent it on supplies. The crew returns to find Usopp, beaten and ashamed. This is Usopp's breaking point.
  6. **Usopp's Challenge** — Harry announces he has decided to get a new ship. Usopp explodes. He accuses Harry of abandoning the Merry, of not caring, of being a bad captain. Harry tries to stay calm but Usopp pushes harder. Finally, Usopp says the words: "If this is how you treat your ship... then I quit. And I challenge you for the Going Merry. Captain vs crew member. A duel." The crew is frozen. Let Harry react to the challenge from his friend.
- **Emotional Beat:** The devastating news about the Merry. Usopp's breakdown. The crew fracturing from within — worse than any enemy.
- **Anime Moments:** The Merry's broken keel revealed, Usopp beaten bloody by the Franky Family, Usopp challenging Harry to a duel
