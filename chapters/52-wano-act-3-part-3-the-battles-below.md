# Chapter 52: Wano (Act 3, Part 3) - The Battles Below

- **Setting:** Onigashima — throughout the fortress
- **Key Characters:** Zoro, Sanji, the crew, King, Queen, Big Mom, Law, Kid
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Zoro vs King** — King the Wildfire — last of the Lunarian race. Zoro must fully conquer Enma. King of Hell Three-Sword Style. Conqueror's Haki coating on all three blades. He cuts King down.
  2. **Sanji's Crisis** — Sanji's Germa genetic modifications are activating. He calls Zoro: "If I lose my emotions — kill me." Zoro: "If you lose yourself, I'll drag you back."
  3. **Sanji vs Queen** — Sanji destroys his Raid Suit — rejecting Germa entirely. Ifrit Jambe — blue fire. He kicks Queen off Onigashima. He is still himself.
  4. **Law and Kid vs Big Mom** — Law's Awakened KROOM attacks from inside. Kid's Awakened magnetism crushes her. They drive her into the magma below. Big Mom falls.
  5. **Big Mom's Defeat** — Two Supernovas brought down an Emperor. The world will never be the same.
- **Emotional Beat:** Sanji destroying his Raid Suit — choosing humanity over power. Zoro mastering Enma. Two rookies defeating an Emperor.
- **Anime Moments:** Zoro's King of Hell three-sword slash, Sanji's Ifrit Jambe, Law and Kid's combined attack
