# Chapter 70: The Final War - Imu

- **Setting:** The Pangaea Castle throne room
- **Key Characters:** Harry, Imu, the Empty Throne
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **The Throne Room** — Harry enters the vast, ancient, silent throne room. Imu sits upon the Empty Throne. "Don't you ever get bored?"
  2. **Imu Speaks** — "Freedom leads to chaos. I am not a tyrant. I am a gardener." Harry: "That's the loneliest thing I've ever heard."
  3. **The Battle Begins** — Imu can erase things from existence. Harry narrowly dodges.
  4. **Gear 5 vs. Erasure** — Creation vs. destruction, freedom vs. control, laughter vs. silence.
  5. **Harry Is Pushed to His Limits** — Imu partially erases Harry's left arm. It reforms through sheer willpower. Harry falls.
  6. **The Voices** — Harry hears voices from everyone he's ever helped. Shirahoshi praying. Momonosuke shouting. Millions of voices from every ocean. "Be free."
  7. **Harry Rises** — The Drums of Liberation thunder back louder than ever. Harry's eyes snap open. He grins: "They said they want to be free."
  8. **The Final Clash** — Harry's fist channels the collective will of everyone who has ever dreamed of freedom. The punch meets Imu's erasure power. Creation vs. destruction. Harry's fist connects.
  9. **Imu Falls** — The Empty Throne shatters. 800 years of age catch up to Imu. "Perhaps... you are what Joyboy was supposed to be."
  10. **Uranus Deactivates** — With Imu's death, Uranus shuts down. "We can proceed with the plan."
  11. **Harry in the Ruins** — Harry stands battered and grinning. "Guess nobody sits there anymore." His legs give out. The crew rushes in.
  12. **The Throne is Empty Forever** — The Empty Throne, which was never truly empty, is now truly gone.
- **Emotional Beat:** Harry hearing the voices of the entire world calling him to stand — "Be free."
- **Anime Moments:** The Drums reigniting, the final punch shattering the Empty Throne, the crew lifting Harry up
