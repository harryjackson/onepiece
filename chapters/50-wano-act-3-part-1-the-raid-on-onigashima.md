# Chapter 50: Wano (Act 3, Part 1) - The Raid on Onigashima

- **Setting:** Onigashima — Kaido's fortress island (a giant skull)
- **Key Characters:** Everyone. The full alliance vs the Beast Pirates and Big Mom Pirates.
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Infiltrating Onigashima** — The alliance sneaks in during the Fire Festival. Kaido's forces are drunk. Chopper drives a tank.
  2. **Kaido's Forces** — All-Stars (King, Queen, Jack), Tobi Roppo (six ancient Zoans), 30,000 soldiers. Outnumbered 6 to 1.
  3. **Jinbe Arrives** — Jinbe walks in and officially joins the crew. Ten members complete.
  4. **Kaido and Big Mom's Plan** — They announce the New Onigashima Project: find the One Piece, obtain the Ancient Weapons, conquer the world.
  5. **The Scabbards Strike** — The Nine Scabbards attack Kaido on the rooftop. Their combined attacks wound him — Kaido can bleed.
  6. **Kaido Defeats the Scabbards** — Kaido's hybrid form defeats all nine. Kiku loses an arm. Ashura takes a fatal wound. Kin'emon is impaled. The torch must be passed.
  7. **Individual Battles** — Franky vs Sasaki. Jinbe vs Who's Who. Robin vs Black Maria — Robin unleashes her demon form. Nami and Usopp vs Ulti and Page One.
  8. **Tama's Power** — O-Tama uses her dango to tame thousands of Kaido's Gifters. The starving girl turns the tide of war.
- **Emotional Beat:** The Scabbards fighting for Oden and falling. Jinbe officially joining. Tama turning the tide.
- **Anime Moments:** The Scabbards attacking Kaido together, Robin's demon form, Jinbe declaring he joined
