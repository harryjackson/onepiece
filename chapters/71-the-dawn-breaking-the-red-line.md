# Chapter 71: The Dawn - Breaking the Red Line

- **Setting:** The Red Line above Fishman Island
- **Key Characters:** Harry (recovering), crew, Shirahoshi, Momonosuke, Sea Kings, Zunesha
- **Scenes:** [PROJECTED]
  1. **Recovery** — Harry wakes up. Chopper: "You are NOT moving! You fought a god!" Harry whines about being hungry.
  2. **The Plan in Motion** — Pluton is ready. Shirahoshi has communed with the Sea Kings. Zunesha has arrived. All three Ancient Weapons in position.
  3. **Shirahoshi's Moment** — "For 800 years, Joyboy promised to bring us to the surface. Today, that promise is kept."
  4. **Momonosuke Fires Pluton** — The blast targets the Red Line at the precise calculated point.
  5. **The Red Line Cracks** — Combined forces converge. Harry delivers a final Gear 5 blow. The Red Line cracks. The loudest sound the world has ever heard. And then it begins to fall.
  6. **The Wall Falls** — Controlled demolition guided by the Ancient Weapons and Sea Kings. The four oceans begin to merge. The All Blue is born.
  7. **Sanji Sees the All Blue** — Fish from all four seas swim together for the first time. Sanji drops to his knees. "The All Blue... it's real. Old man Zeff... it's real."
  8. **Fishman Island Rises** — The Sea Kings carry Fishman Island upward through the gap. Sunlight hits directly for the first time. Children see clouds for the first time.
  9. **Jinbe's Promise Kept** — "Otohime... Tiger... we did it. We're in the sun."
  10. **The World Transforms** — The Calm Belts destabilize. The Grand Line normalizes. The barriers that divided the world are falling.
  11. **Harry Watches** — "Did Joyboy... is this what he wanted?" Robin: "This is exactly what he wanted. You kept his promise, Harry."
  12. **The Dawn** — The sun rises through the gap in the Red Line. The first sunrise ever visible from this angle. A new era. A new dawn.
- **Emotional Beat:** Sanji seeing the All Blue — the impossible dream that Zeff called impossible. It's real.
- **Anime Moments:** The Red Line cracking, Fishman Island rising into sunlight, the sun rising through the gap
