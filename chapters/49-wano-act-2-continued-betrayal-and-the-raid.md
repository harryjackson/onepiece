# Chapter 49: Wano (Act 2 continued) - Betrayal and the Raid

- **Setting:** Wano
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Law, Kid, the Scabbards, Kaido, Big Mom
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Traitor Revealed** — Kanjuro is revealed as a spy for Orochi. The meeting port is destroyed. It appears the rebellion has failed before it started.
  2. **Harry Arrives Anyway** — Harry, Law, and Kid sail to the port regardless. Harry will not abandon the plan.
  3. **The Fleet Appears** — Out of the storm, sails appear. 5,400 warriors, sailing for Onigashima despite everything. The Scabbards weep.
  4. **Two Emperors** — Big Mom has formally allied with Kaido. The odds are impossible. Harry does not care.
  5. **Kanjuro's Cruelty** — Kanjuro kidnaps Momonosuke. A man they trusted for twenty years was their enemy.
  6. **The Raid Begins** — The fleet sails into Onigashima's waters. The biggest battle in One Piece history begins.
- **Emotional Beat:** The fleet appearing in the storm despite everything. Kanjuro's betrayal.
- **Anime Moments:** The samurai fleet emerging from the storm, Kanjuro revealing his betrayal, the alliance sailing toward Onigashima
