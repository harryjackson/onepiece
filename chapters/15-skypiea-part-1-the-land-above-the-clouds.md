# Chapter 15: Skypiea (Part 1) - The Land Above the Clouds

- **Setting:** Skypiea — an island in the sky at 10,000 meters
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Conis, Pagaya, Gan Fall (Sky Knight), Enel (God), the Shandians (Wyper)
- **Key Events:**
  - The crew arrives in Skypiea — a cloud island with its own people, culture, and history
  - Meet the sky people, Conis and her father Pagaya
  - Learn about "God" Enel — a tyrant who rules Skypiea with an iron fist and claims to be God. Anyone who defies him is destroyed by lightning
  - Enel ate the Rumble-Rumble Fruit (lightning Logia) — considered one of the most powerful Devil Fruits in existence
  - The Shandians — the original inhabitants — are fighting a 400-year war to reclaim their homeland (Upper Yard), which is actually part of Jaya launched into the sky centuries ago
  - The crew enters Upper Yard — a survival game set by Enel where everyone must fight to survive
  - Individual crew battles against Enel's priests (Satori, Shura, Gedatsu, Ohm)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The White Sea** — The crew lands on clouds. Actual clouds you can walk on (Island Clouds) and swim in (Sea Clouds). The Going Merry floats on a cloud sea. Everything is white and ethereal. Chopper, Usopp, and Harry immediately jump into the cloud sea — it is like soft cotton. They find sky fish, sky creatures, and Dials (shell-shaped devices that store and release energy, sound, light, or impact).
  2. **Heaven's Gate** — An old woman at the gate charges them to enter. Harry walks past without paying. This is now a crime in Skypiea. The entire crew is technically illegal. Let Harry realize the sky has its own laws — and he has already broken them.
  3. **Conis and the Angel Beach** — They meet Conis and her father Pagaya — kind sky people with small wings on their backs. Conis plays a harp and shows them the wonders of Skypiea: Waver boats (cloud jet-skis), Dial-powered appliances, cloud homes. Harry tries to ride a Waver and crashes into everything. Nami masters it instantly (she is a navigator genius). Let Harry enjoy the wonder of the sky island.
  4. **God Enel** — The crew learns the horrifying truth. "God" Enel rules Skypiea as a tyrant. He can hear everything on the island using Observation Haki (called "Mantra" here). He controls lightning — the Rumble-Rumble Fruit, considered one of the most powerful Devil Fruits in existence. Anyone who defies him is struck by lightning from the sky. The people live in total fear. Even mentioning resistance means death.
  5. **The Shandians** — The original inhabitants of the land — the Shandians — have been fighting for 400 years to reclaim their homeland (Upper Yard), which is actually the missing half of Jaya, launched into the sky centuries ago. Their warrior leader, Wyper, is fierce, relentless, and willing to die for his ancestors' land. The conflict between sky people and Shandians has raged for generations.
  6. **Upper Yard — The Survival Game** — Enel declares a survival game. Everyone on Upper Yard — crew, Shandians, his own priests — must fight. He predicts only five will survive. The jungle is deadly, filled with giant snakes, traps, and Enel's four priests (Satori, Shura, Gedatsu, Ohm), each with unique Dial-powered combat styles.
  7. **The Crew Splits and Fights** — The crew battles through Upper Yard. Zoro fights Ohm (sword trials with iron cloud). Sanji encounters Satori's swamp ordeal. Chopper and Nami face traps. Usopp fights despite his terror. Gan Fall — the former God of Skypiea, now a knight riding a flying horse — helps the crew. Each priest uses Dials in creative combat (Impact Dials that absorb and release force, Flame Dials, Axe Dials). Let Harry fight through the jungle and encounter the bizarre challenges of sky combat.
  8. **Robin and the Poneglyph** — Robin discovers the ruins of the Golden City of Shandora — the ancient city Noland found 400 years ago. It is here, in the sky. She finds a Poneglyph with ancient text. She reads it quietly, learning about the true history. This is what Robin lives for — the truth of the world, hidden in stone.
- **Emotional Beat:** The wonder of the sky island contrasted with Enel's tyranny. The 400-year war between sky people and Shandians.
- **Anime Moments:** The crew arriving on clouds, Enel's lightning striking from the sky, Robin finding the Poneglyph in the Golden City ruins
