# Chapter 57: Egghead Island (Part 1) - The Island of the Future

- **Setting:** Egghead Island — Dr. Vegapunk's laboratory complex, 500 years ahead of its time
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Vegapunk (Stella and six satellites), Jewelry Bonney, Kuma (Pacifista form), Sentomaru
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Arrival at the Future** — Gleaming metal structures, holograms, floating platforms. Franky is practically crying with joy.
  2. **The Warm Eddy and the Giant Shark** — A massive mechanical shark attacks. Bonney appears — furious and half-starved, looking for answers about her father Kuma.
  3. **Meeting Bonney** — Age-Age Fruit. She's here for revenge on Vegapunk for turning Kuma into a weapon.
  4. **The Island's Layers** — Fabriophase below (scrapyard), Labophase above (floating lab). Harry wants to go straight to the top.
  5. **Atlas — The Fist of Destruction** — One of Vegapunk's six satellite bodies. She immediately picks a fight with Harry.
  6. **Lilith's Ambush** — The "Evil" satellite tries to rob the Straw Hats with Mecha-Sharks.
  7. **Ascending to the Labophase** — The crew rides a vacuum tube elevator to the floating laboratory.
  8. **Vegapunk Stella Appears** — The real Vegapunk — absurdly giant head, absentminded, brilliant. He's been waiting for someone who carries the will of Joyboy.
  9. **The Ancient Robot** — A massive 900-year-old robot from the Void Century. Harry feels something when he touches it.
  10. **Bonney's Rage** — Bonney sees Kuma's memories — a kind father turned into a weapon. She attacks Vegapunk. Vegapunk says Kuma asked for this.
  11. **York's Scheme (Seeds)** — York secretly communicates with someone. First hint of a traitor.
  12. **Evening on Egghead** — The crew eats dinner. Vegapunk looks at them with a sad, knowing expression.
- **Emotional Beat:** Bonney seeing Kuma's memories — the gentle father she lost, turned into a weapon.
- **Anime Moments:** First view of Egghead Island, Bonney touching Kuma's memory bubble, the ancient robot's eyes flickering
