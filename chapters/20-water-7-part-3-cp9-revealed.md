# Chapter 20: Water 7 (Part 3) - CP9 Revealed

- **Setting:** Water 7, Galley-La headquarters
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, CP9 (Rob Lucci, Kaku, Kalifa, Blueno), Franky
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Masks Come Off** — At Galley-La headquarters, the truth is revealed. Rob Lucci — the quiet, pigeon-holding carpenter — is CP9's strongest agent. Kaku — the friendly long-nosed shipwright — is CP9. Kalifa — Iceburg's secretary — is CP9. Blueno — the bartender — is CP9. Five years of perfect cover, shattered in an instant.
  2. **Rob Lucci** — Lucci is terrifying. Leopard-Leopard Fruit (Zoan type) — he transforms into a leopard-human hybrid. He is a master of Rokushiki — six superhuman martial arts. He is cold, emotionless, and lives to kill. He defeats Harry with contemptuous ease. Finger Pistol through Harry's chest. Harry goes down.
  3. **CP9 Takes Robin and Franky** — CP9 captures Robin (who surrendered willingly) and Franky (who they want for knowledge of Pluton's blueprints). They board the sea train headed for Enies Lobby. The crew is beaten, scattered, and broken.
  4. **Franky's Backstory** — Franky is revealed to be Cutty Flam — the apprentice of the legendary shipwright Tom. Tom built Gol D. Roger's ship, the Oro Jackson. Tom was put on trial by the World Government. Young Franky tried to stop the sea train with his body — and was destroyed. He rebuilt himself as a cyborg. He has Tom's blueprints for Pluton. Let Harry learn Franky's story and feel the connection to Roger.
  5. **The Crew Recovers** — Bruised, bleeding, humiliated. But Harry stands up. "We're going to get Robin back." There is no debate. No hesitation. They are going to Enies Lobby.
  6. **Sanji on the Train** — Sanji boarded the sea train ahead of everyone, alone. He fights through cars full of Government agents, Marine escorts, and CP9 operatives. One man against an army, on a moving train in the rain. He is magnificent.
  7. **The Ride to War** — The crew boards the next sea train — Rocketman, a prototype that goes too fast and cannot stop. They ride it through the storm toward Enies Lobby. On the train with them: Galley-La shipwrights, the Franky Family, and determination. They are heading to war with the World Government.
- **Emotional Beat:** The betrayal of trusted allies. Rob Lucci's terrifying power. The crew's determination to save Robin despite everything.
- **Anime Moments:** Rob Lucci transforming into his leopard form, Sanji fighting alone through the sea train, the Rocketman charging through the storm
