# Chapter 60: Egghead Island (Part 4) - The Escape

- **Setting:** Egghead Island shores, the surrounding sea, aboard the Thousand Sunny
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Dorry and Brogy (Giant Warrior Pirates), the Gorosei, Kizaru, Bonney, Kuma, the ancient robot
- **Scenes:**
  1. **Fighting Retreat** — The crew battles through the crumbling Labophase. Every step toward the shore is paid for in blood.
  2. **Bonney's Awakening** — Bonney transforms into a Nika-like appearance. She blasts Saturn backward.
  3. **The Ancient Robot Awakens** — The 900-year-old Iron Giant fully activates. It attacks the Gorosei with ancient power.
  4. **Kuma Arrives** — A mindless Pacifista that walked across the ocean. No mind, no memories. But his body shields Bonney from Saturn. Love is the one thing the World Government couldn't erase.
  5. **Kuma vs. Saturn** — Kuma keeps coming. Every time Saturn knocks him down, he stands back up.
  6. **Harry vs. Kizaru — Final Exchange** — Harry lands a massive blow. Kizaru mutters, "You really have surpassed us all, haven't you?"
  7. **The Giants Arrive** — Dorry and Brogy on giant longships. "LITTLE FRIENDS! WE HEARD YOU NEEDED A RIDE!"
  8. **Dorry and Brogy's Combo Attack** — Hakoku Sovereignty — a shockwave that splits the sea.
  9. **Boarding the Giant Ship** — The Sunny is loaded onto a giant ship. Atlas and Stussy stay behind.
  10. **Vegapunk's Final Words** — "The world will sink. Find the One Piece. It holds the key to everything." The broadcast cuts out. Vegapunk dies.
  11. **The World Reacts** — Quick cuts to reactions worldwide. Dragon: "It's time."
  12. **Sailing Toward Elbaf** — Harry: "We're going to find the One Piece. And then we're going to change the world."
- **Emotional Beat:** Kuma walking across the ocean for Bonney — a mindless machine driven by a father's love.
- **Anime Moments:** Kuma stepping between Bonney and Saturn, Dorry and Brogy's Hakoku Sovereignty, Vegapunk's final broadcast
