# Chapter 61: Elbaf (Part 1) - The Island of Giants

- **Setting:** Elbaf — the legendary warrior island of giants, home of the world's strongest warriors
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Dorry, Brogy, Hajrudin, Loki, Usopp (major focus), Shanks (presence felt)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **First Sight of Elbaf** — An island of impossible scale. Yggdrasil — the World Tree — towering into clouds. Usopp starts crying before they dock.
  2. **Welcome by the Warriors** — The giants throw an enormous welcome feast. Harry nearly drowns in a giant's mug of ale.
  3. **Usopp's Pilgrimage** — Usopp visits the Hall of Warriors. A giant elder says: "You are the small warrior with the long nose who never backed down?" Usopp stands tall. "That's me."
  4. **The Warrior Trials** — Traditional tests of strength, courage, and honor. The crew participates.
  5. **The Story of Prince Loki** — Loki is imprisoned for crimes against the giant kingdom. He knows where the final Road Poneglyph is.
  6. **Loki's Cell** — Loki offers to trade information for freedom. Harry's instincts scream not to trust him.
  7. **Shanks's Shadow** — Shanks was recently on Elbaf. His message: "I'm waiting for you at the end."
  8. **Bonney and Kuma at Elbaf** — The giants recognize Kuma as a Buccaneer. A healer says a faint spark remains within him.
  9. **Robin at the Library of Giants** — Robin finds texts from the Void Century. "The treasure of the world is not gold. It is truth."
  10. **The Feast of Warriors** — Harry wins an eating contest against three giants. Usopp tells stories the giants believe.
  11. **The Night Watch** — Harry and Jinbe sit atop Yggdrasil. Harry: "I just want everyone to be free to eat what they want and go where they want."
  12. **The Road Poneglyph Clue** — Robin discovers the final Road Poneglyph is connected to Elbaf itself. Blackbeard's crew has been spotted nearby.
- **Emotional Beat:** Usopp walking through the Hall of Warriors and beginning to see himself as the brave warrior he's always wanted to be.
- **Anime Moments:** Yggdrasil towering into the clouds, Usopp before the Hall of Warriors, the great hall singing Binks' Sake
