# Chapter 43: Zou - The Moving Island

- **Setting:** Zou — a kingdom built on the back of a 1,000-year-old elephant walking through the sea
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Law, the Mink tribe (Inuarashi, Nekomamushi, Carrot, Pedro), Sanji (absent)
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Walking Island** — Zou is not an island — it is a kingdom built on Zunesha, a colossal elephant walking through the ocean for 1,000 years.
  2. **The Mink Tribe** — The Minks are animal-people who all know Electro. Rulers are Inuarashi (day) and Nekomamushi (night). They meet Carrot and Pedro.
  3. **The Devastation** — Zou has been devastated. Jack — one of Kaido's All-Star commanders — attacked looking for Raizo. He unleashed poison gas. The Minks nearly died but never gave up Raizo.
  4. **"Raizo Is Safe!"** — The Minks reveal Raizo alive and declare with tears: "Raizo is safe! We would never betray a comrade!" The samurai weep. Loyalty between nations.
  5. **The Road Poneglyphs** — Robin discovers the Minks guard one of four Road Poneglyphs that reveal the location of Laugh Tale. Four needed. The race to the end begins.
  6. **Sanji Is Missing** — Sanji has been taken by Big Mom's crew. He's been forced into a political marriage. Sanji is secretly Vinsmoke Sanji — a prince of Germa 66, a military kingdom of genetically enhanced super-soldiers. He ran away as a child.
  7. **The Crew Splits** — Harry takes a group to Whole Cake Island to get Sanji back. Zoro takes a group to Wano to prepare for the rebellion.
  8. **Harry Hears Zunesha** — Harry hears Zunesha's voice — deep, ancient. Only Harry and Momonosuke can hear it. Harry can hear the voice of all things. Just like Roger could.
- **Emotional Beat:** "Raizo is safe!" — loyalty between nations. Sanji's painful past revealed. Harry hearing Zunesha's voice.
- **Anime Moments:** The Minks revealing Raizo, Zunesha crushing Jack's fleet, the crew splitting
