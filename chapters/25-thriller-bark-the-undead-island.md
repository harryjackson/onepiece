# Chapter 25: Thriller Bark - The Undead Island

- **Setting:** Thriller Bark — a massive ship/island in the Florian Triangle, covered in fog and monsters
- **Key Characters:** Harry, crew, Brook, Gecko Moria (Shadow-Shadow Fruit), Oars, Bartholomew Kuma
- **Scenes:**
  1. **The Florian Triangle** — The Sunny sails into dense fog. Ghost ships drift past. A skeleton walks onto their deck: "May I see your panties?" This is Brook.
  2. **Brook** — He ate the Revive-Revive Fruit. When he died his soul returned but his body was bones. Alone for 50 years. Cheerful, polite, skull jokes. He cannot join — his shadow was stolen.
  3. **Thriller Bark** — The largest ship in the world. Halloween-themed. Gecko Moria, Shadow-Shadow Fruit. He steals shadows and creates zombies. Without your shadow, sunlight dissolves you.
  4. **Shadows Stolen** — Moria steals Harry's shadow, puts it into Oars — a giant hundreds of feet tall with Harry's personality. Harry must avoid sunlight.
  5. **Brook's Backstory** — His crew, the Rumbar Pirates, promised baby Laboon they would return. They all died. Their last song — Binks' Sake — was recorded on a Tone Dial, voices fading as each one dies mid-song. Brook has been alone 50 years carrying it.
  6. **The Crew vs Oars** — The entire crew fights Oars together. Zoro cuts, Sanji kicks, Franky shoots, Nami controls weather, Robin uses her arms. First true combined battle.
  7. **Harry vs Moria** — Harry absorbs 100 shadows (Nightmare Harry). Demolishes Moria. Moria falls.
  8. **Kuma Appears** — Bartholomew Kuma. Warlord. Paw-Paw Fruit. The exhausted crew cannot fight him.
  9. **Zoro's Sacrifice** — Zoro offers his life for Harry's. Kuma extracts all of Harry's pain into a bubble. Zoro steps in. Every wound floods his body. Sanji finds him in a pool of blood. "What happened?" Zoro: "Nothing happened."
  10. **Brook Joins** — Shadow recovered. He plays Binks' Sake on his violin. The crew sings along. He will return to Laboon. Nine members.
- **Emotional Beat:** "Nothing happened." Zoro's sacrifice. Brook's 50-year solitude. Binks' Sake.
- **Anime Moments:** The Rumbar Pirates singing as they die, Zoro standing in blood, Brook playing violin on the Sunny
- **Crew After Chapter:** Harry, Zoro, Nami, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook
