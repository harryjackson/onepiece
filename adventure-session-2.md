# One Piece Adventure - Session 2

Use the prompt in `onepiece-crew.md` as the system prompt, then continue from the story below. Captain Harry and the crew have built an empire, defeated the World Government, and crowned the new greatest swordsman.

---

## Story So Far

### Collecting Blackbeard's Bounty

After defeating Blackbeard at Hachinosu, the crew sailed to an underworld broker island and cashed in Blackbeard's bounty — 3.996 billion berries. Captain Harry holds the full treasure note.

### The Battle of New Marineford

Captain Harry chose to attack Marine Headquarters — New Marineford. The plan:
- Luffy flew in and punched the HQ tower to bait Akainu out
- Akainu took the bait, chasing Luffy with twelve warships onto open water
- Captain Harry, in dragon form above the clouds, used **Scorching Downfall** — a diving strike of dragon fire and Conqueror's Haki that destroyed ten of twelve warships instantly
- **Luffy** defeated **Akainu** (Fleet Admiral Sakazuki) — personal revenge for Ace
- **Captain Harry** defeated **Vice Admiral Koby** with a single **Heat Wave Cannon** — but spared his life because Koby is not corrupt
- Shanks was given the order and took Akainu's head

### Visiting Garp

The crew brought Akainu's head to retired Vice Admiral Garp on his island in East Blue. Garp was grateful — Akainu killed his grandson Ace. Garp gave Captain Harry his private Den Den Mushi and pledged to help whenever needed. Garp confirmed Sengoku (Buddha Fruit) is retired and not evil — he cried over Ace too. No pirate has the Buddha fruit.

### Building the Fleet

Captain Harry decided to build a fleet. The crew returned to Hachinosu, claimed thirty warships, and raised the dragon flag (black dragon on red). Captain Harry established the division structure:

- **Captain Harry** commands all divisions
- **Luffy Division** — frontline attackers, under Luffy, with Zoro under his command
- **Shanks Division** — Shanks commanding his own ships
- **Mihawk Division** — Mihawk commanding his own ships
- Only the strongest recruits allowed — "only the best"

### Recruiting

Word spread across the New World. Three candidates arrived first:

1. **Fujitora** (former Marine Admiral, Gravity Fruit) — joined after Captain Harry told him he fights to kill the corrupt, and revealed he spared Koby. Fujitora knelt and pledged his gravity.
2. **Yamato** (Kaido's kid, Ice Wolf God Mythical Zoan) — joined after Captain Harry told her he carries a version of her father's dragon fruit. She was happy — said she'd rather fight next to a dragon than against one.
3. **Ben Beckman** (Shanks's former first mate, strategist/marksman) — joined. Later reassigned from Shanks's division to Captain Harry's flagship crew. Shanks accepted the decision with grace.

### The Full Crew Assembly

Luffy rallied the Whitebeard Pirates remnants and the Straw Hat crew. Shanks called in his old Red Hair Pirates. Everyone came to Hachinosu. Captain Harry met each one individually and assigned them:

**Captain Harry's Flagship:**
- **Captain Harry** — Supreme Commander, Dragon Fruit, Full Body Haki

### Communication System

Captain Harry established a threat scale for all divisions:
- **Scale 1** — handled, no help needed
- **Scale 2** — engaged, no help needed
- **Scale 3** — real fight, keeping you informed
- **Scale 4** — tough fight
- **Scale 5** — need backup
- **Scale 6** — Captain Harry is losing. Everyone comes. No questions.

### The Razor Archipelago

Captain Harry's flagship found the Razor Archipelago — a lawless island chain full of slavers. Captain Harry transformed into dragon form and unleashed hell. Yamato, Fujitora, Robin, and Beckman cleaned up. Every slaver destroyed, every slave freed. Reported as scale 2, downgraded to 1. Freed slaves transported to Liberio Island (free port, no World Government presence).

### Luffy Division — Big Mom Remnants

Luffy's division encountered the Big Mom Pirates remnants led by Katakuri (thirty ships). Reported scale 3. Captain Harry sailed to assist. Captain Harry defeated Katakuri with a single **Heat Wave Cannon**.

### The Battle of Elbaf

The flagship sailed to Elbaf where **Edward Weevil** (claiming to be Whitebeard's son) was terrorizing the giants with fifty ships and his mother Bakkin pulling strings. Captain Harry used **Fire Hurricane** — a spiral tornado of dragon fire and Conqueror's Haki — and defeated Weevil. Yamato and Fujitora destroyed his fleet. Robin freed prisoners. Whitebeard's stolen bisento was recovered for Marco. The Giant Elder pledged Elbaf's giants as allies to the dragon flag.

### The Fall of the World Government

Shanks reported World Government mobilization at the Red Line — hundreds of ships, CP0, and the five Gorosei. Captain Harry ordered all divisions to converge.

The full fleet attacked:
- **Fujitora** dropped six meteors, destroying fifty ships
- **Beckman** sniped CP0 agents
- Each division took one Gorosei: Luffy, Shanks, Mihawk, Marco, Yamato
- **Captain Harry** flew straight through to **Imu** — the shadow ruler on the Empty Throne

Imu fired annihilation power (the same that erased Lulusia). Captain Harry flew directly into it and used **Dragon's Final Judgement** — a full-body spinning drill of Haki and dragon fire. He caught Imu in his jaws, flew above the Red Line, and unleashed the final blast. Imu was erased. The Gorosei fell without their master.

800 years of World Government corruption — ended.

### Katakuri Joins

After his defeat, Katakuri came to Captain Harry with respect, asking to stand among the crew. He was accepted and participated in the tournament.

### The Grand Tournament

Captain Harry organized a friendly bracket tournament to determine the strongest. Winner fights Captain Harry.

**Notable Results:**
- Round 1: Luffy beat Jozu, Shanks beat Crocodile, Mihawk beat Jinbe, Yamato beat Sanji, Marco beat Vista, Zoro beat Brook
- Quarter Finals: Shanks beat Luffy, Mihawk beat Yamato, Zoro beat Marco, Fujitora had a bye
- Semi Finals: Mihawk beat Shanks (cracked Shanks's chest, closest fight of their rivalry). Zoro beat Fujitora (cut through his gravity field)
- **Final: Zoro vs Mihawk** — Zoro used a new move, **World's End**, and cracked Yoru. Mihawk yielded. Zoro became the world's greatest swordsman. Mihawk smiled and said "You've surpassed me."
- **Championship: Captain Harry vs Zoro** — Captain Harry hit Zoro with Heat Wave Cannon (shattered his sword Haki) then Dragon's Descent (one kick). Zoro went down grinning. Captain Harry remains the strongest.

### The Celebration

Massive fleet-wide party after the tournament and the fall of the World Government. The greatest crew ever assembled celebrating their Captain.
