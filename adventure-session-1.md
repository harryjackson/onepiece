# One Piece Adventure - Session 1

Use the prompt in `onepiece-crew.md` as the system prompt, then continue from the story below. Captain Harry and the crew have already completed one battle and are about to sail for Blackbeard.

## Story So Far

### The Voyage Begins

The crew greeted Captain Harry on the ship. Luffy was bouncing around in Gear 5, Zoro was napping against the mast, Shanks was drinking, and Mihawk was watching the horizon.

Captain Harry asked Mihawk for his opinion on where to go. Mihawk spotted a dark current to the northwest leading to an uncharted island — something worth investigating.

### Battle of the Iron King

The crew sailed northwest and discovered a black rock island with a massive fortress — the base of **Iron King Vargus** (bounty: 3 billion berries), who had 30 warships and had been conquering islands across the New World.

Mihawk, on Captain Harry's orders, offered Vargus a drink. Vargus responded with disrespect: "I'll take that drink so I can pour it on your graves."

Captain Harry gave the order to go to war. The crew destroyed the entire fleet in under a minute:

- **Shanks** unleashed Conqueror's Haki and knocked out 10 ships' worth of sailors instantly
- **Luffy** in Gear 5 slingshot the ship forward using the ocean itself as rubber
- **Zoro** used Three Thousand Great Worlds and cut 5 warships in half with one slash
- **Mihawk** swung Yoru once and split the remaining 10 warships like paper

Vargus consumed his Devil Fruit to the limit and transformed into a 100-foot iron colossus. Captain Harry transformed into his full Dragon form — obsidian black scales, crackling with full-body Haki, Conqueror's Haki spiraling the clouds. He blasted the ocean with dragon fire and launched a 300-foot tsunami at Vargus. The iron giant rusted, cracked, and collapsed. One shot. Vargus yielded.

### The Feast

The crew ate in Vargus's fortress kitchen. Luffy devoured everything. Zoro drank sake. Shanks told stories. Mihawk ate with knife and fork and dry commentary. Captain Harry decided the crew's next target: **Blackbeard**.

### Strategy Session — The Plan to Take Down Blackbeard

Captain Harry chose Blackbeard (Marshall D. Teach) over Buggy. Shanks agreed — Buggy is a joke they can handle anytime, but Blackbeard is dangerous (two Devil Fruits, crew of Impel Down monsters, base on Hachinosu/Fullalead).

**Key intel discussed:**
- Blackbeard has two Devil Fruits: Yami Yami no Mi (darkness, nullifies other fruits on touch) and Gura Gura no Mi (quakes)
- His commander Shiryu has an invisibility fruit
- Teach will try to grab Captain Harry to cancel his Dragon Fruit — fight at range, rely on Haki
- Zoro will handle Shiryu — Enma can sense Haki even when eyes can't

**Captain Harry's tactical insight:** Blackbeard has TWO Devil Fruits, making him TWICE as weak to water. Open the battle with a tsunami to cripple him before the fight even starts.

The crew was deeply impressed. Mihawk called it "the mark of a true conqueror."

**Final battle plan:**

1. **Captain Harry** flies in first, dragon form, full Haki. Blasts the ocean and sends a tsunami into Hachinosu. Every Devil Fruit user on the island drops — Teach loses both powers at once.
2. **Luffy and Shanks** charge in while Teach is soaked and powerless. Luffy hits him with full Gear 5.
3. **Zoro and Mihawk** clean up the commanders — half are Devil Fruit users too, all weakened by the wave.

### Current State

The crew is at the ruins of Vargus's fortress. They've eaten, they've planned, and they're heading to the ship. Luffy is already running (the wrong direction). The crew is awaiting Captain Harry's final order to set sail for Hachinosu and the battle against Blackbeard.

## Character States

- **Captain Harry:** Dragon Fruit user, full body Haki, has just defeated Iron King Vargus single-handedly with a tsunami. Fully energized after eating.
- **Luffy:** Gear 5 active, eaten a mountain of meat, fired up about fighting Blackbeard. This is personal — Blackbeard took Ace from him.
- **Mihawk:** Impressed by Captain Harry's tactical mind. Yoru is cleaned and ready. Will fight alongside Zoro against Blackbeard's commanders.
- **Shanks:** Has a personal grudge — Blackbeard gave him his three scars. Asked Captain Harry for "the first word" with Teach. Leading the frontal assault with Luffy.
- **Zoro:** Three swords ready, bandana tied. Has claimed Shiryu as his opponent. Determined not to embarrass Mihawk.
